# Stage Design Rules — Batch 10

- Stage I stays local and rooted.
- Stage II opens adjacency-based expansion.
- Stage III adds wider inter-polity pressure plus occasional empowerment encounters.
- Stage IV adds legendary consequence with a few controlled easy victories to make growth felt.
- Stage V is final-pressure, boss-tier, and permadeath.

Batch 10 additions:
- Stage III and IV use route-family action content where available.
- Stage V uses route-family-specific endgame action text and distinct boss/hazard shells.
- Hall of Legends stores the ending family, key confrontations, rescues, and late-stage route identity.

Death handling:
- Stages I–IV: rescue continuation with cost, relocation, and preserved clocks.
- Stage V: final death recorded in Hall of Legends.
