# Ledger of Ash — How to Play

## Starting

1. Open index.html in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
2. Choose "Begin New Legend" to start a new run
3. Enter your name, a 4-digit save passcode, your class, and your background
4. Your legend begins in your starting location

## Saving

Your progress saves automatically using your 4-digit passcode.
To load a previous legend: enter your passcode on the title screen and press Enter.

## Controls

- Click/tap choices to make decisions
- Use the top bar buttons to access: Map, Journal, NPCs, Notices, Party, Camp, Sheet
- Camp to rest, train, and talk with companions
- Hall of Legends preserves all completed runs

## Class Families

**Classic Combat** (Warrior, Knight, Ranger, Paladin, Archer, Berserker, Warden, Warlord, Death Knight)
Direct force, weapon mastery, threat control, protection

**Magic and Spellcasting** (Wizard, Cleric, Priest, Necromancer, Illusionist, Inquisitor, Elementalist, Oracle)
Pattern reading, elemental authority, ritual solving, arcane evidence

**Stealth and Precision** (Rogue, Assassin, Spellthief, Scout, Thief, Trickster, Beastmaster)
Infiltration, surveillance, shadow economy, terrain reading

**Support and Leadership** (Healer, Artificer, Engineer, Tactician, Alchemist, Saint, Bard)
Community networks, technical analysis, coordination, chronicle

## Settings

No additional setup required. Browser-playable, no server needed.
Works on desktop and mobile browsers.
