# Ledger of Ash — itch.io Description & Metadata

## Title
Ledger of Ash — A Text-First TTRPG Fantasy Game

## Short Description
Craft your legend through 31 archetypes, 93 unique backgrounds, and 5 stages of escalating narrative consequence. Every choice writes your ledger.

## Long Description

**Ledger of Ash** is a text-first, single-player TTRPG-style fantasy game where every archetype and background combination tells a distinct story.

### Create Your Legend
Choose from **31 unique archetypes** across 4 classes:
- **Combat** (Warrior, Knight, Ranger, Paladin, Archer, Berserker, Warden, Warlord, Death Knight)
- **Magic** (Wizard, Cleric, Priest, Necromancer, Illusionist, Inquisitor, Elementalist, Oracle)
- **Stealth** (Rogue, Assassin, Spellthief, Scout, Thief, Trickster, Beastmaster)
- **Support** (Healer, Artificer, Engineer, Tactician, Alchemist, Saint, Bard)

Each archetype has 3 backgrounds (Civic, Frontier, Occult) for **93 unique origin stories**.

### Progress Through 5 Stages

**Stage I (Levels 1–4): Grounding**
Investigate a local contradiction in your origin locality. Read the pressure. Find the hidden hand.

**Stage II (Levels 5–8): Adjacent Widening**
Push outward along a widening route. Uncover faction pressure, NPC motivations, and world-changing consequences.

**Stage III–V: Regional & Legendary**
Unlock family edges, face elite creatures, decide who controls the escalating pressure of memory, transport, contamination, guild power, forbidden knowledge, industrial violence, legal authority, or sacred rites.

### Master the Systems
- **8 Route Families**: Each with distinct pressure mechanics and skill-check content
- **10 Localities**: Shelkopolis, Panim Haven, Sunspire Haven, and more—each with unique culture and mechanical pressure
- **5 Skill Trees**: Combat, Survival, Persuasion, Lore, Stealth, Craft—develop your character's strengths
- **20 Creatures & 20 Hazards**: From Scarab Swarms to Corrupted Archons; from Steep Collapses to Plague Spores
- **Class Synergy**: Recruit companions whose skills stack with yours; form a party or walk alone
- **Persistent World**: Your choices reshape settlement services, NPC opinions, and route accessibility

## Installation Instructions

**Play Online**:
1. Download and extract the itch.io build
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
3. Start a new legend or load a saved game

**Play Locally**:
- Windows: `python -m http.server 8001` then open `http://localhost:8001`
- Mac/Linux: `python3 -m http.server 8001` then open `http://localhost:8001`

**Play on a Static Host**:
- Upload `dist/index.html` to any static hosting service
- Open the URL in a browser
