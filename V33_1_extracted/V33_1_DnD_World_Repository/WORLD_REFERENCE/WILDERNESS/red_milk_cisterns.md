# Red Milk Cisterns

**Region:** Phoenix Island / Salted Peninsula

**Why it matters:** A chain of rock cisterns that turn certain algae blooms pink after hard storms. Travelers prize the water; locals prize controlling who gets to call it communal.

**What players notice first:** A chain of rock cisterns that turn certain algae blooms pink after hard storms.

**Current tensions:** water rights, poison fears, oasis trade.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
