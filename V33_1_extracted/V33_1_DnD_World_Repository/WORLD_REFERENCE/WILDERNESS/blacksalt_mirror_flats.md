# Blacksalt Mirror Flats

**Region:** The Principalities / Roaz Frontier

**Why it matters:** A dead-silent salt basin that reflects torchlight seconds before torches are raised. Prison labor crews avoid it after dusk because voices seem to arrive from ahead of their speakers.

**What players notice first:** A dead-silent salt basin that reflects torchlight seconds before torches are raised.

**Current tensions:** magic dead zone, contraband burial, reflection omens.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
