# Copper Jackal Steppe

**Region:** Nomdara Circuits / Cross-Continental Caravan Reach

**Why it matters:** Dry grassland crossed by abandoned wheel-ruts that reappear after rain. Caravan children say the land remembers every bargain made on it.

**What players notice first:** Dry grassland crossed by abandoned wheel-ruts that reappear after rain.

**Current tensions:** caravan bandits, ancestral debt, grazing conflict.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
