# Gallows Orchard Of Hardfruit

**Region:** Phoenix Island / Northern Harbor Coast

**Why it matters:** An inland orchard where wind-hardened fruit grows on trees bent into execution-shape by old storms. Families picnic nearby anyway during hardness festivals.

**What players notice first:** An inland orchard where wind-hardened fruit grows on trees bent into execution-shape by old storms.

**Current tensions:** festival site, local dare, hidden meetings.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
