# Choir Ice Breach

**Region:** Sheresh Communes / Glasswake Expanse

**Why it matters:** A singing crevasse that harmonizes with nearby spellcasting and makes novice cantrip users dangerously brave. Nearby communes post listeners before they post guards.

**What players notice first:** A singing crevasse that harmonizes with nearby spellcasting and makes novice cantrip users dangerously brave.

**Current tensions:** arcane lure, rescue site, mutation risk.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
