# Cinder Wind Sinks

**Region:** Soreheim Alliance / Outer Wastes

**Why it matters:** A field of ash funnels that inhale sound before spitting it out miles away. Stone giants use them to pass warnings; raiders use them to stage fear.

**What players notice first:** A field of ash funnels that inhale sound before spitting it out miles away.

**Current tensions:** ambush acoustics, weather omen, giant signaling.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
