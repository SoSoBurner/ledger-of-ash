# Mothershard Icewell

**Region:** Sheresh Communes / Far Northern Tundra

**Why it matters:** A vertical well of black-blue ice said to descend toward older contamination seams and older truths about the skies. Scholars debate it; smugglers map it.

**What players notice first:** A vertical well of black-blue ice said to descend toward older contamination seams and older truths about the skies.

**Current tensions:** forbidden research, descent hazard, rare reagents.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
