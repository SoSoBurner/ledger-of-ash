# Dreadwake Shoals

**Region:** The Principalities / Cosmouth Approaches

**Why it matters:** A reef shelf where wreckage drifts back against the tide days after storms. Cosmouth factors quietly pay for anything bearing intact seals or ledgers.

**What players notice first:** A reef shelf where wreckage drifts back against the tide days after storms.

**Current tensions:** salvage law, ghost stories, courier losses.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
