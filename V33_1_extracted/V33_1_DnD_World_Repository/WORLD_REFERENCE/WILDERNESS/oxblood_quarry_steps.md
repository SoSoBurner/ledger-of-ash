# Oxblood Quarry Steps

**Region:** Soreheim Alliance / Western Extraction Line

**Why it matters:** A terraced quarry stained red by mineral runoff and old labor deaths. The lower steps still produce superior stone at a social price nobody agrees to count.

**What players notice first:** A terraced quarry stained red by mineral runoff and old labor deaths.

**Current tensions:** resource pressure, labor unrest, structural collapse.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
