# Aurora Tithe Fields

**Region:** Sheresh outer tundra

**Why it matters:** A radiant contamination band where harvested light is taxed, painted, and sometimes stolen before it can be rendered safe.

**What players notice first:** A radiant contamination band where harvested light is taxed, painted, and sometimes stolen before it can be rendered safe.

**Current tensions:** mutation risk, private ownership, illegal harvesters.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing the fields alters exposure risk and can entangle the party in local property law.

**Use in play:** Put one environmental obstacle, one social read, and one consequence for being seen here before the party exits the scene.
