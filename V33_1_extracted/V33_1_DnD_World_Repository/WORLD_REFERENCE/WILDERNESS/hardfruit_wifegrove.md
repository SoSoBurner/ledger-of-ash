# Hardfruit Wifegrove

**Region:** Phoenix Island / Prosperity Verge

**Why it matters:** An orchard-court where household disputes are cooled through gift tasting, witness testimony, and controlled public humiliation. Outsiders often mistake the laughter for safety.

**What players notice first:** An orchard-court where household disputes are cooled through gift tasting, witness testimony, and controlled public humiliation.

**Current tensions:** inheritance pressure, witness theater, concealed feuds.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
