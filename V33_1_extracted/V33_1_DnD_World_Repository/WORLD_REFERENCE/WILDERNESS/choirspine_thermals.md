# Choirspine Thermals

**Region:** Sheresh Communes / Northern Dome March

**Why it matters:** Steam fissures cut through blue ice and turn every spoken word into layered echoes. Pilgrims come to test omens here, but smugglers use the noise to hide movement between communes.

**What players notice first:** Steam fissures cut through blue ice and turn every spoken word into layered echoes.

**Current tensions:** omen site, echo cover, thermal hazard.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and which local interest learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
