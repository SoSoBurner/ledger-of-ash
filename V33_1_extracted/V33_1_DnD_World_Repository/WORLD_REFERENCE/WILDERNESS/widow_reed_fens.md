# Widow Reed Fens

**Region:** The Principalities / Zootia Marsh Edge

**Why it matters:** Tall reedwater where floodgrain caravans vanish one axle at a time. The fen keeps footprints poorly but keeps gossip very well.

**What players notice first:** Tall reedwater where floodgrain caravans vanish one axle at a time.

**Current tensions:** smuggling, marsh predators, hidden ferries.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
