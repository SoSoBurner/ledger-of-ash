# Queenfall Scar

**Region:** Phoenix Island / SE Prosperity

**Why it matters:** A ravine lined with old war banners turned to mineral husks. When war-law stories rise again, the ravine fills with listeners pretending they just came for shade.

**What players notice first:** A ravine lined with old war banners turned to mineral husks.

**Current tensions:** martial memory, treasure rumors, household politics.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
