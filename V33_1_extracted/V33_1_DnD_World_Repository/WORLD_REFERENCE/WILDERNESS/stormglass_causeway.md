# Stormglass Causeway

**Region:** The Principalities / Shelk Coastline

**Why it matters:** A broken imperial road fused into glass ribs by repeated storm strikes. Couriers still risk it because the old route cuts hours from travel when the sky is willing.

**What players notice first:** A broken imperial road fused into glass ribs by repeated storm strikes.

**Current tensions:** fast route, lightning hazard, message interception.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
