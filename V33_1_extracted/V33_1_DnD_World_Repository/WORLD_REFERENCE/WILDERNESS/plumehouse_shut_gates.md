# Plumehouse Shut Gates

**Region:** The Principalities / Shelk Road March

**Why it matters:** An abandoned roadwarden gatehouse whose wardrobe vault still holds rotting bright plumes and sealed travel logs. Patrols deny using it, which is how everyone knows they still do.

**What players notice first:** An abandoned roadwarden gatehouse whose wardrobe vault still holds rotting bright plumes and sealed travel logs.

**Current tensions:** buried witness logs, wanted-poster risk, patrol embarrassment.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
