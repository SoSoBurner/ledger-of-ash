# Widowsalt Causeway

**Region:** Phoenix Island / Interior Causeways

**Why it matters:** A salt-white road raised over brine flats where returning husbands leave gifts at household markers and rival matriarchies quietly measure one another's losses.

**What players notice first:** A salt-white road raised over brine flats where returning husbands leave gifts at household markers and rival matriarchies quietly measure one another's losses.

**Current tensions:** ritual road, house rivalry, brine hazard.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and which local interest learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
