# World Overview

The world is organized around divine fracture, doomsday aftermath, politicized trade, magical contamination, harsh regional cultures, and pressure-bearing travel.

## Primary macroregions
- **The Principalities:** house rule, roads, archives, tariffs, prisons, trade friction, and House-vs-Union legitimacy battles.
- **Sheresh Communes:** cold magical civilization under contamination pressure, experimentation, dome protection, and art-infused daily life.
- **Soreheim Alliance:** tower industry, interdependent labor castes, massive infrastructure, giant governance, and production risk.
- **Phoenix Island / Psanan-linked hardness culture:** matriarchal households, vow logic, war memory, gift economy, and severe social expectations.
- **Nomdara circuits:** moving trade sovereignty, neutral bargaining, and route knowledge as power.

## GM use
Start with polity tension, then choose a locality, then route pressure, then one NPC who wants something costly.
