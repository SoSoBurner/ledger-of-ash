# V33_1 Next Canon-Safe Content Priority Analysis

## Governing conclusion
current release does **not** most urgently need broader generalized worldspread. The repository is already broadly playable at the world layer. The greater need is deeper operational content that turns existing canon into a stronger play surface.

The safest, highest-value additions are:
- more starter packets
- filled campaign persistence files
- stronger locality-to-route-to-pressure crosslinks
- more faction primers
- more rumor packs
- more offscreen consequence tracking
- current-campaign packets for House Shelk vs the Union

This analysis treats the package as standalone and repository-governed. It preserves asserted canon exactly, avoids unsupported fact invention, and favors play usefulness, continuity stability, and retrieval reliability over ornamental expansion.

---

## Priority 1: starter packets for already surfaced localities
The highest-priority need is starter packets for remaining surfaced localities that already exist as named, navigable world objects but do not yet have full zero-to-scene support at the operator surface.

### Best targets
- Arbiters Row
- Ashforge Citadel
- Ashforge Quarry
- Aurora Crown Commune
- Cosmoria
- Fairhaven
- Harvest Keep
- Hullwake Foundry
- Market Crossroads
- Mimolot Academy
- Panim Haven
- Shirshal
- Silent Haven
- Sunspire Haven
- Unity Square
- Witness Coil

These are high-value because they are already asserted locations and can be deepened without inventing unsupported canon. Their arrival kits, escalation ladders, quickstart cards, locality packets, and in many cases route and travel assets already exist. The missing work is not world invention but operator surfacing.

### Production-ready standard for each starter packet
Each packet should directly point to:
- its arrival kit
- its escalation ladder
- its route card(s)
- its travel complication(s)
- 2 to 4 relevant named NPCs
- 1 active pressure
- 1 nearby fallback scene
- 1 consequence propagation note

This is canon-safe because it improves usability of asserted content rather than inventing new truth layers.

## Priority 2: campaign persistence content at the root
The live continuity layer is structurally present but underfilled. The following files were placeholders and needed real entries derived from play:
- CURRENT_CAMPAIGN_STATE.md
- CHANGED_LOCALITIES.md
- ACTIVE_FACTIONS.md
- UNRESOLVED_PRESSURES.md
- NPCS_THE_PARTY_DISTURBED.md

This is especially important because the active campaign sheet already establishes a live scenario: **House Shelk vs the Union**, in **Shelkopolis**, with the party objective to **find the daughter of Lord Myrs Shelk**, along with named NPCs and active faction pressure.

The package stops feeling like a living campaign engine when these files exist only as empty scaffolds. Once populated, they become the root continuity surface that tells a DM what is live now.

---

## Priority 3: crosslink density
Crosslink density remains too thin in the places where the DM most needs immediate handoff. Every starter locality should explicitly bridge:
- locality
- route
- travel complication
- faction
- named NPC surface
- active pressure
- fallback scene
- consequence propagation note

This is the safest kind of deepening because it binds already asserted supports into a runnable sequence rather than inventing a new truth layer.

---

## Priority 4: faction-primer parity
Full faction-primer parity is still needed. The highest-value faction targets are:
- House Shelk
- The Union
- Roadwardens Order
- Red Hood Guild
- Great House Citadel Circle
- Magi Magistratus
- Sheresh Dome Stewards
- Agricultural Syndicate
- Engineers Consortium
- House-level remnant claimants and enforcement bodies where already asserted

Each primer should make clear:
- what the faction wants
- how it operates
- what it fears
- what it is doing now
- how it reacts to party interference

Faction usability is still one of the biggest multipliers on table quality because factions are how canon pressure becomes play.

---

## Priority 5: rumor starter expansion
Rumor starter expansion is needed by polity and route, especially for locality clusters and major road or harbor corridors where travel, trade, suspicion, or tension matter.

Rumors are high-value because they activate existing canon without forcing hard retcons. They let the world move in the players’ perception before the players commit to a lane.

Highest-yield rumor surfaces next:
- Shelkopolis–Fairhaven lane
- Guildheart Hub–Unity Square–Market Crossroads corridor
- Cosmoria harbor ring and connected inland freight
- Shirshal and Witness Coil investigative corridor
- Panim Haven memorial and custody route
- Soreheim freight and quarry corridors
- Sheresh dome approach lanes

---

## Priority 6: consequence propagation and offscreen world motion
Consequence propagation and offscreen world motion remain underdeveloped. This is what turns the package from a static archive into a living campaign engine.

Offscreen progression should track:
- which faction benefits if the party delays
- what changes in markets, registries, communes, quarries, or courts after incidents
- which NPCs become more hostile, frightened, or opportunistic
- which roads become safer or more dangerous over time

The repository already contains many locality and faction anchors. What it needs now is the connective runtime layer that records how those objects move when the party does nothing, chooses a side, or leaves.

---

## Priority 7: authored inside packets for civic institutions
More authored “inside” packets are needed for civic institutions, especially:
- registries
- courts
- guild halls
- shrines and temples
- quarries and foundries
- estate compounds
- checkpoints and harbormaster spaces

These are valuable because they support immediate play when characters walk into meaningful institutions. They prevent the DM from hitting a dead surface exactly when the players move from broad locality context into decision-heavy room-level play.

## Final conclusion
current release needs more authored **launch surfaces** and **continuity surfaces**, not more generalized worldspread.

The safest and highest-value additions are:
- more starter packets
- filled campaign persistence files
- stronger locality-to-route-to-pressure crosslinks
- more faction primers
- more rumor packs
- more offscreen consequence tracking
- current-campaign packets for House Shelk vs the Union
