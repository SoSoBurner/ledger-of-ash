# Ashforge Citadel

**Polity:** Psanan

**Arrival:**** Black-red ramparts, forge smoke, and ash-banner civic markers announce a city accustomed to heat, rebuilding, and rule.

**Inside:**** Citadel courts, dedication halls, memorial arches, and weapon forges sit beside plazas made for public processions and ember dances.

**Economic role:** weapons forging, civic metallurgy, matriarchal administration

**Law and enforcement feel:** forge discipline, rebuilding vow enforcement, House precedence law

**Religious and cultural texture:** ancestral volcanic honor is visible, while renewal custom remains civic and household rather than temple doctrine

**Active tensions:** house rivalry, forge accidents, ashfall damage

**Named NPC slice:** Ashra Ashvein, Forge-Voice Malzara, Ina Cinderbrace, Matriarch Ashvara of the Citadel Circle, Matron Heshka Emberreign, Mother Saar Vulkrel, Vatra Sul, Vekka Ashvein

**Linked routes:** Ashforge Citadel - Ashwake harbor lane, Ashforge Citadel - Scarlet Descent road, Ashwake Port - Ashforge Citadel harbor road

**World-bridge block:** Ashforge Citadel matters because it anchors Psanan in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
