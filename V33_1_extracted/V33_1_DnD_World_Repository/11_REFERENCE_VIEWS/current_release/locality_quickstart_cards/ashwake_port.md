# Ashwake Port

**Polity:** Psanan

**Arrival:**** Dark quays, soot-streaked cranes, and renewal-marked public ways guide travelers through a port built to recover quickly from damage.

**Inside:**** Port ledgers, storage vaults, ash-dye houses, and processional squares give the harbor both commerce and ceremony.

**Economic role:** trade, ash dye, metal export, ship resupply

**Law and enforcement feel:** port tariff law, public way clearance during observances

**Religious and cultural texture:** renewal-marked banners serve civic identity and public recovery rather than worship

**Active tensions:** harbor ash clogging, storm-whirlpool risk, port rivalry

**Named NPC slice:** Cindri Ashvein, Morra Ashvein, Port Factor Nemra Sootwake

**Linked routes:** Ashwake Port - Ashforge Citadel harbor road

**World-bridge block:** Ashwake Port matters because it anchors Psanan in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
