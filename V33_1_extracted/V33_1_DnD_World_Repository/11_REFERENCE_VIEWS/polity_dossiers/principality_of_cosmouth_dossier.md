**Dominant pressure pattern:** storm weather
**Common outsider mistake:** Treating Cosmoria like a casual stop rather than a governed space.
**How power really moves:** Through House Cosmouth.
**Best first three nodes to stage:** Cosmoria, Hullwake Foundry, Tideglass Registry
**Named NPC depth:** 24 records tied to this polity in V30_3.
