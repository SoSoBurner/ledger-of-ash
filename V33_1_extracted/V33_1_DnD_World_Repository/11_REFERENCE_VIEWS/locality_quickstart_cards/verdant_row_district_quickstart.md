# Verdant Row District quickstart card

**Polity:** Shelkopolis
**Visual read:** Colorful awnings, looms, hammers, flower boxes, baked bread, spiced tea, and merchant traffic.
**Behavioral read:** value work, collaboration, and practical courtesy
**Danger read:** economic fraud; design theft; route security
**Who matters first:** Fashion Artisans Collective, Roadwarden command staff, academy instructors
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Verdant Row District immediately highlights the industrious artisan and guild district of shelkopolis
