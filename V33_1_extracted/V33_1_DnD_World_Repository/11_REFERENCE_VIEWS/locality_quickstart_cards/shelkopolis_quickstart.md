# Shelkopolis quickstart card

**Polity:** Principality of Shelk
**Visual read:** Pale stone walls rise from meadows with golden accents, indigo and emerald banners, and flower-lined roads watched by bright-plumed Roadwardens.
**Behavioral read:** observe site-specific decorum; respect local fashion and craft traditions; follow Roadwarden road and market order; treat shrines and archives with reverence
**Danger read:** glyph corruption beyond the city; trade security obligations; noble rivalry; festival traffic
**Who matters first:** House Shelk nobility, Roadwardens Order, Fashion Artisans Collective
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Shelkopolis immediately highlights largest metropolis of shelk and one of the major urban engines of the principalities, defined by fashion, trade, refined civic order, and visible devotional plurality
