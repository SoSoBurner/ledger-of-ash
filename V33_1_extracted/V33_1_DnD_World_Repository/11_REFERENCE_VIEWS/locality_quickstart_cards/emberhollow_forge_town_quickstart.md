# Emberhollow Forge Town quickstart card

**Polity:** Psanan
**Visual read:** Cooled black stone terraces, open furnace yards, and rebuilt retaining walls frame the settlement.
**Behavioral read:** honor forge schedules; maintain shared heatworks
**Danger read:** furnace failure; labor disputes; ash burial
**Who matters first:** Forge Dedication Halls, Frontier Hammer Companies
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Emberhollow Forge Town immediately highlights a forge-centered town built on older burn layers where prestige comes from making durable goods from former ruin
