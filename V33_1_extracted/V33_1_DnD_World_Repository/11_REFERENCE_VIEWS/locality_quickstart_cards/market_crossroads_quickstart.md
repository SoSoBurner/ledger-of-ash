# Market Crossroads quickstart card

**Polity:** The Union
**Visual read:** Wheel-rut geometry, ferry callouts, stacked relay barns, bonded storehouses, and cramped loading courts make it obvious that nothing here exists without movement and managed turnover.
**Behavioral read:** respect relay order; do not block lanes; pay storage and crossing fees promptly
**Danger read:** route bottlenecks; bandit intelligence leakage; cargo spoilage; licensed smuggling
**Who matters first:** route wardens, Union clerks, carrier guilds
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Market Crossroads immediately highlights an urbanized route-knot settlement where road, river, and guild carriage systems exchange goods, rumor, and obligation through dense freight procedure
