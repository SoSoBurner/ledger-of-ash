# Fairhaven quickstart card

**Polity:** Principality of Shelk
**Visual read:** A practical town framed by fields and market traffic, with access roads feeding cave-bound travelers and farm carts.
**Behavioral read:** respect local purification rites; heed warning about celestial creatures and cave anomalies
**Danger read:** glyph corruption; celestial creatures at Watcher’s Perch; suspicious travelers in taverns
**Who matters first:** local clergy, market elders, crafters linked to Shelkopolis
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Fairhaven immediately highlights agricultural and artisanal town supporting shelkopolis while sitting near active glyph-cave pressure
