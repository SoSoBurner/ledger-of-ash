# Sealwater Conclave quickstart card

**Polity:** The Principalities
**Visual read:** Visitors meet courtyards, seal rooms, and controlled waiting spaces built to make impatience costly.
**Behavioral read:** wait to be called; do not interrupt sealed process
**Danger read:** agenda backlog; precedence disputes
**Who matters first:** conclave clerks, house delegates
**Easiest playable complication:** minor precedence insult
**Best opener:** Sealwater Conclave makes confederated power feel negotiated rather than unified.
