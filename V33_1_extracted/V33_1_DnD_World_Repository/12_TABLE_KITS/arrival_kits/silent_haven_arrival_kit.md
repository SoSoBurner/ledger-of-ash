# Silent Haven Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Fortified coastal settlement shaped by watchfulness and maritime necessity.

**First pressure:** sea threat, northern route vulnerability

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Silent Haven.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with sea threat, northern route vulnerability.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Silent Haven is covering a more expensive conflict tied to Principality of Roaz.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Helm Steelmarch
