# Market Crossroads Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Wheel-rut geometry, ferry callouts, stacked relay barns, bonded storehouses, and cramped loading courts make it obvious that nothing here exists without movement and managed turnover.

**First pressure:** route bottlenecks, bandit intelligence leakage, cargo spoilage, licensed smuggling

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Market Crossroads.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with route bottlenecks, bandit intelligence leakage, cargo spoilage, licensed smuggling.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Market Crossroads is covering a more expensive conflict tied to The Union.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Calia Ledgermere
