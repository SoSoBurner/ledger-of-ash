# Scarlet Descent Gate Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Winch towers, red-lit vent shafts, and hardened gatehouses signal a place built to go downward and return altered.

**First pressure:** shaft collapse, lava surge, crew loss

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Scarlet Descent Gate.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with shaft collapse, lava surge, crew loss.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Scarlet Descent Gate is covering a more expensive conflict tied to Psanan.
