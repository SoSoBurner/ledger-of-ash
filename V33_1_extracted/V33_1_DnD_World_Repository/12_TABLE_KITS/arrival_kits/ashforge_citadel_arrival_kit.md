# Ashforge Citadel Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** Black-red ramparts, forge smoke, and ash-banner civic markers announce a city accustomed to heat, rebuilding, and rule.

**First pressure:** house rivalry, forge accidents, ashfall damage

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Ashforge Citadel.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with house rivalry, forge accidents, ashfall damage.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Ashra Ashvein, Forge-Voice Malzara, Ina Cinderbrace, Matriarch Ashvara of the Citadel Circle, Matron Heshka Emberreign, Mother Saar Vulkrel, Vatra Sul, Vekka Ashvein
