# Craftspire Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** Tall workhouses, copy towers, materials yards, ledger balconies, and cart-choked service lanes rise around a district where skilled labor and regulated output become visible prestige.

**First pressure:** copy theft, intellectual property disputes, material shortages, sabotage

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Craftspire.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with copy theft, intellectual property disputes, material shortages, sabotage.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Jorin Ledgermere, Tess Ledgermere, Union Burdened Clerk 4, Union Dockside or Street Fixer 5, Union Factor 2, Union Gate Marshal 1, Union Licensed Appraiser 8, Union Ritual Custodian 3
