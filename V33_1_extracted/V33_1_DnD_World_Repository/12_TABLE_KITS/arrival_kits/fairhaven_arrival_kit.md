# Fairhaven Arrival Kit

**First visual:** A practical town framed by fields and market traffic, with access roads feeding cave-bound travelers and farm carts.

**First pressure:** glyph corruption, celestial creatures at Watcher’s Perch, suspicious travelers in taverns

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Fairhaven.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with glyph corruption, celestial creatures at Watcher’s Perch, suspicious travelers in taverns.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Fairhaven is covering a more expensive conflict tied to Principality of Shelk.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Corin Bloomcrest
