# Panim Haven Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Temples, ritual logistics, and funerary commerce shape the city.

**First pressure:** divine balance obligations

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Panim Haven.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with divine balance obligations.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Panim Haven is covering a more expensive conflict tied to House Panim.
