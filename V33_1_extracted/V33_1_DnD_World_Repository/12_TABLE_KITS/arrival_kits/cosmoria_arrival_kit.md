# Cosmoria Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** Grand libraries and maritime infrastructure define first impressions.

**First pressure:** storm weather, trade vulnerability

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Cosmoria.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with storm weather, trade vulnerability.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Aurek Tidereach, Coral Jack Neris, Coralyn Foamglass, High Tide-Priest Coren Mirthwake, Ilyra Foamveil, Maris Coralwake, Nerissa Bluefin, Niv Brinegrin
