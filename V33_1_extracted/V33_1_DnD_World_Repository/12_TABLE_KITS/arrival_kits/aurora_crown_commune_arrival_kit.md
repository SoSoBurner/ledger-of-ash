# Aurora Crown Commune Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** Blue-white dome glow, layered ice walls, and guarded thermal inlets announce a commune built to keep life possible.

**First pressure:** dome stress, contamination seepage, ration balancing

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Aurora Crown Commune.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with dome stress, contamination seepage, ration balancing.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Neren Rimebridge, Talin Rimebridge, Vassa Rimebridge, Warden Sera Whiteglass
