# Roaz Northern Trade Route Travel Complications

**Core route identity:** Roaz Northern Trade Route

**Checkpoint problem:** Travel along Roaz Northern Trade Route is easiest when intentions, cargo, and patronage are legible before questions begin. Now make one of those suddenly unclear.

**Patrol complication:** Daylight oversight is visible and procedural; night oversight is thinner, more suspicious, and less forgiving. The party arrives at the wrong patrol moment.

**Night complication:** After sunset, traffic thins, trust narrows, and informal power brokers matter more than posted rules. Someone offers a shortcut with a future cost.

**Seasonal pressure:** weather delays, supply interruption, inspection backlog

**Hazard twist:** pirates, hostile polities, winter limits
