# Principality of Zootia route corridor 3 Travel Complications

**Core route identity:** Principality of Zootia route corridor 3

**Checkpoint problem:** Documents help, but recognizable sponsorship or useful clarity matters just as much. Now make one of those suddenly unclear.

**Patrol complication:** Routine by day; selective and sharper after dark. The party arrives at the wrong patrol moment.
