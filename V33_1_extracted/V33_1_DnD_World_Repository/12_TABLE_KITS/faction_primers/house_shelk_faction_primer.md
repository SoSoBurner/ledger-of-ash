# House Shelk Faction Primer

**Identity:** House Shelk

**What it wants:** Keep trade secure, fashion prestigious, and Shelk visibly harmonious without surrendering political leverage.

**How it operates:** patronage, route order, noble diplomacy, cultural sponsorship

**What it fears:** Harmony is part of statecraft, so embarrassment and public disorder hit House Shelk harder than blunt scarcity alone.

**What it is doing now:** route safety, counterfeits, glyph anxieties, noble intrigue

**How it reacts to party interference:** If the party helps, it will try to formalize that help into obligation. If the party obstructs it, it will answer through the same methods it uses to preserve leverage.

**Best first conflict:** A routine request becomes factional the moment the party changes pace, access, or witness control in a space this faction treats as governed.
