# Sunspire Haven quickstart card

**Polity:** Soreheim Alliance
**Visual read:** Broad roads, cart yards, workshops, and counting sheds gather around a busy market heart in productive country.
**Behavioral read:** contribute and trade fairly; show respect to the family or syndicate controlling the yard you stand in; carry sealed papers when moving labor or grain
**Danger read:** predators; water system vulnerabilities; storage disputes; family favor feuds
**Who matters first:** regional leaders, market stewards, workshop syndicates
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Sunspire Haven immediately highlights rural crossroads market town where tools, textiles, grain contracts, and labor obligations move through workshop syndicates and patron-families
