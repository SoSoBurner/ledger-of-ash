# Serpent Measure quickstart card

**Polity:** Principality of Zootia
**Visual read:** The district appears calm until a traveler notices how many eyes are counting movement rather than admiring abundance.
**Behavioral read:** stay in marked lanes; answer quantity questions clearly
**Danger read:** shortfall anxiety; coercive bookkeeping
**Who matters first:** House Zootia, reserve clerks
**Easiest playable complication:** delayed clearance
**Best opener:** Serpent Measure announces itself through the visible logic of reserve ledgers.
