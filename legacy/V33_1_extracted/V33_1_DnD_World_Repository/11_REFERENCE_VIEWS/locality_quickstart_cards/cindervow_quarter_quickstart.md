# Cindervow Quarter quickstart card

**Polity:** Psanan
**Visual read:** Layered burn-stone walls, ash-wing banners, and engraved arches show repeated destruction and deliberate rebuilding.
**Behavioral read:** speak plainly in oath spaces; do not interrupt dedication work or memorial processions
**Danger read:** rebuilding backlog; house competition over visibility; structural heat stress
**Who matters first:** Rebuilding Oath Courts, Forge Dedication Halls
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Cindervow Quarter immediately highlights prestigious rebuilt quarter known for surviving destruction, swearing public vows, and turning fire damage into civic memory
