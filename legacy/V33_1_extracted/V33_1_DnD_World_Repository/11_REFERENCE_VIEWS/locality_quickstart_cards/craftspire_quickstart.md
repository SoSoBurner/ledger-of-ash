# Craftspire quickstart card

**Polity:** The Union
**Visual read:** Tall workhouses, copy towers, materials yards, ledger balconies, and cart-choked service lanes rise around a district where skilled labor and regulated output become visible prestige.
**Behavioral read:** respect queue and workshop boundaries; do not touch commissioned work; observe tariff marking rules
**Danger read:** copy theft; intellectual property disputes; material shortages; sabotage
**Who matters first:** The Union, copying offices, licensed guild houses
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Craftspire immediately highlights highly urbanized production district where artisan output, book-copy bureaucracy, and licensed knowledge trade converge into industrial economic authority
