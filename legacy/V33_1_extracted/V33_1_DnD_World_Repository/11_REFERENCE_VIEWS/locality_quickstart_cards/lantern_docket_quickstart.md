# Lantern Docket quickstart card

**Polity:** Principality of Shirsh
**Visual read:** Visitors notice intake doors, waiting benches, and the unnatural calm of a place built to absorb panic.
**Behavioral read:** answer directly; respect case order
**Danger read:** backlog pressure; misclassification
**Who matters first:** Magi Magistratus, docket clerks
**Easiest playable complication:** delayed clearance
**Best opener:** Lantern Docket announces itself through the visible logic of filing work.
