# Unity Square

**Polity:** The Union

**Arrival:**** Paved exchange courts, stacked counting houses, covered loading lanes, tally towers, and color-coded guild awnings make the square feel like a civic market machine rendered in stone, wood, and cloth.

**Inside:**** Voices overlap across a managed density of bargaining floors, inspection sheds, carrier lanes, vendor rows, and sanctioned dispute corners surrounded by constant urban movement.

**Economic role:** market mediation, spot trade, inspection fees, transport matching

**Law and enforcement feel:** Market fraud and sanction breaches are publicly punished to preserve confidence.

**Religious and cultural texture:** Small shrines exist at the edges, but market rhythm dominates the center.

**Active tensions:** price shocks, cargo theft, route closures, public rumor flareups

**Named NPC slice:** Vale Brokerwell, Vale Ledgermere, Vale Tinmarch

**Linked routes:** Guildheart Hub - Unity Square bargaining road

**World-bridge block:** Unity Square matters because it anchors The Union in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
