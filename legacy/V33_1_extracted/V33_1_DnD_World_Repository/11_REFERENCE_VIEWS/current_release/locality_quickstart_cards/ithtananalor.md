# Ithtananalor

**Polity:** Principality of Roaz

**Arrival:**** Austere walls, disciplined checkpoints, and industrial silhouettes announce a city built for law and production.

**Inside:**** Fortresses, foundries, tribunal logic, prison labor systems, and administrative command centers set the civic rhythm.

**Economic role:** ore control, legal infrastructure, technology, prison labor logistics

**Law and enforcement feel:** Roazian antimagic and order law saturate public life.

**Religious and cultural texture:** Justice-aligned civic order dominates public symbolism more than overt softness.

**Active tensions:** illicit magic, smuggling, public accountability for harsh enforcement

**Named NPC slice:** Casvel Redhinge, Clerk Oth Brenn, Dame Orsella Roaz, Marshal-Builder Korrin Wex, Marshal Sera Ironveil, Mira Steelmarch, Orren Pike, Rhodra Ironspike

**Linked routes:** Ithtananalor - Ironhold Quarry road, Ithtananalor - Silent Haven convoy lane

**World-bridge block:** Ithtananalor matters because it anchors Principality of Roaz in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
