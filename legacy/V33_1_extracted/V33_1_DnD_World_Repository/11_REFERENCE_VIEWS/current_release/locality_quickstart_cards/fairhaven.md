# Fairhaven

**Polity:** Principality of Shelk

**Arrival:**** A practical town framed by fields and market traffic, with access roads feeding cave-bound travelers and farm carts.

**Inside:**** Shrines, schoolcraft, small enchantment shops, taverns, and gossip-rich inns define the town’s rhythm.

**Economic role:** food supply, alchemical ingredients, enchanted tools, minor arms and textiles

**Law and enforcement feel:** standard Shelk road and market law; increased concern for dangerous magic and public safety near glyph anomalies

**Religious and cultural texture:** Felujitas and Cyfoes are visible through chapel and fountain worship.

**Active tensions:** glyph corruption, celestial creatures at Watcher’s Perch, suspicious travelers in taverns

**Named NPC slice:** Corin Bloomcrest, Elandra Velvetmere, Tavian Silkgate

**Linked routes:** Fairhaven - Shelkopolis road, Shelkopolis - Fairhaven road

**World-bridge block:** Fairhaven matters because it anchors Principality of Shelk in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
