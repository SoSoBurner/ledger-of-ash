# Guildheart Hub

**Polity:** The Union

**Arrival:**** Multi-story counting halls, bonded warehouse blocks, canal-side loading lanes, registry towers, and arbitration pavilions announce that cargo paper, inspection rhythm, and freight infrastructure rule this place.

**Inside:**** Clerks, brokers, arbiters, haulers, and guarded petitioners move through dense administrative floors, storage courts, and hearing chambers where guild insignia matters more than noble heraldry.

**Economic role:** arbitration, tariff mediation, warehouse registration, trade sanctioning

**Law and enforcement feel:** Contract and sanction logic are publicly legible., Failure to register goods or hearings attracts rapid administrative consequences.

**Religious and cultural texture:** Public devotion is present but subordinated to civic contract rhythm.

**Active tensions:** legacy Mal claims, smuggling, Red Hood rumor presence, imperial oversight

**Named NPC slice:** Guild Auditor Bren Oss, Guilden Ledgermere, Halv Ledgermere, Marren Toll, Mira Ledgermere, Old Marrow Jex, Porter Ledgermere, Quen Larkstamp

**Linked routes:** Guildheart Hub - Craftspire workshop corridor, Guildheart Hub - Unity Square bargaining road

**World-bridge block:** Guildheart Hub matters because it anchors The Union in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
