# Chokepoint Dependency Map

The world becomes living when chokepoints do more than delay wagons. Each chokepoint below controls legitimacy, memory, survival, or bargaining tempo.

## High-value chokepoints

### Tideglass Registry
If this falters, ownership, inheritance, bonded cargo confidence, and house legitimacy all destabilize at once.

### Canal Thorn Exchange gates
If these fail, grain confidence collapses faster than actual food stocks. The panic matters as much as the shortage.

### Mimolot Academy tariff rooms
If these are compromised, the question becomes not just what leaked, but who can still claim to control dangerous knowledge.

### Guildheart Hub bargaining chambers
If these lock up, industrial peace becomes private coercion and route delays begin to feel intentional.

### Whitebridge / Domeway seal corridors
If these are damaged or mistrusted, entire communal rhythms become survival triage.

### Ironroot Crossing
If this crossing is threatened, alliance freight, escorts, and strategic mobility all shrink at once.

## GM propagation rule
When a chokepoint changes, propagate effects in this order:
1. immediate local inconvenience or fear
2. institutional response
3. rumor distortion
4. route repricing or delay
5. faction opportunism
6. long-tail legitimacy damage
