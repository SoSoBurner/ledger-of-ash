# Psanan Primer

**Identity:** Volcanic island polity of matriarchal Great Houses, forge industry, underworld exploration, endurance custom, and cyclic renewal under harsh ash and heat conditions.

**Law feel:** Ashra Ashvein, Ashra Cindercrest, Ashra Emberlash, Ashra Glassmelt, Cindri Ashvein, Cindri Cindercrest

**Authority feel:** Ashra Ashvein, Ashra Cindercrest, Ashra Emberlash, Ashra Glassmelt, Cindri Ashvein, Cindri Cindercrest

**Risk feel:** Pressure rises when outsiders ignore procedures, patronage, or corridor expectations.

**Trade feel:** Use the key localities as the fastest trade and leverage map.

**Rumor feel:** Start rumors from trade strain, household ambition, or corridor pressure instead of abstract lore.

**What a newcomer notices first:** Prepare this polity through its corridors, gate routines, and locality tensions rather than treating it as a lore blob.

**Best first locality:** Ashforge Citadel

**Best first conflict:** Put the party into a routine obligation that becomes political once they choose speed, force, or favoritism.
