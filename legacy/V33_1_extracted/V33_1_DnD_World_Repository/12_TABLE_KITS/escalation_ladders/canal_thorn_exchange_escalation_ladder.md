# Canal Thorn Exchange Escalation Ladder

1. **Social tension:** hoarding rumors, canal sabotage
2. **Institutional attention:** A local official documents the problem and narrows acceptable options.
3. **Economic consequence:** Trade, access, or lodging becomes more expensive or slower.
4. **Border restriction:** The party’s movement, paperwork, or cargo gets flagged.
5. **Faction escalation:** A household, broker ring, militia, shrine authority, or civic actor tries to convert the situation into leverage.
