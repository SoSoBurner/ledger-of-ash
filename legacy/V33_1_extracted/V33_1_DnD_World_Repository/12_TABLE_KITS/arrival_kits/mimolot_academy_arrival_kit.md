# Mimolot Academy Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Scholarly prestige and guarded magical infrastructure.

**First pressure:** tariff tensions

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Mimolot Academy.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with tariff tensions.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Mimolot Academy is covering a more expensive conflict tied to House Mimolot.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Archivist Temeris Quillward
