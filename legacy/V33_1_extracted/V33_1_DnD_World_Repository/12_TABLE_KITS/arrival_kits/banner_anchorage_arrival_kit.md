# Banner Anchorage Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** Approach through tiered berths and banner poles makes coalition politics visible before any one authority speaks.

**First pressure:** berth competition, escort favoritism

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Banner Anchorage.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with berth competition, escort favoritism.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Principalities Dock Factor 2, Principalities Escort Broker 3, Principalities Harbor Marshal 1
