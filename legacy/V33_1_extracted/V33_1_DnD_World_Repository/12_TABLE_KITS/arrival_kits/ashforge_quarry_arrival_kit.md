# Ashforge Quarry Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** High walls, ore lifts, beacon chains, and blackened roadbeds cut through a rocky valley alive with hauling noise and watch calls.

**First pressure:** predators, avalanches, geothermal bursts, sabotage, crew rivalries

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Ashforge Quarry.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with predators, avalanches, geothermal bursts, sabotage, crew rivalries.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Dema Coalward, Gorath Myrn, Gryndal Vesskar, Hildran Wyvernharn, Korin Ashmason, Tharim Coalforge, Varric Icevein
