# Sunspire Haven Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Broad roads, cart yards, workshops, and counting sheds gather around a busy market heart in productive country.

**First pressure:** predators, water system vulnerabilities, storage disputes, family favor feuds

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Sunspire Haven.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with predators, water system vulnerabilities, storage disputes, family favor feuds.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Sunspire Haven is covering a more expensive conflict tied to Soreheim Alliance.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Garren Forgebound
