# Aurora Heights District Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** Terraced hill, gilded mansions, jasmine and citrus, noble crests, and quiet fountains.

**First pressure:** alliances, espionage, elite reputation

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Aurora Heights District.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with alliances, espionage, elite reputation.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Aelric Velvetmere, Liora Bloomcrest, Maren Starbloom
