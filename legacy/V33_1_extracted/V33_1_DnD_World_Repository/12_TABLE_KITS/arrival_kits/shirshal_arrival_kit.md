# Shirshal Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Arcane scrutiny and guarded curiosity.

**First pressure:** magical law violations

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Shirshal.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with magical law violations.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Shirshal is covering a more expensive conflict tied to House Shirsh.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Examiner Prelate Sira Doveshade
