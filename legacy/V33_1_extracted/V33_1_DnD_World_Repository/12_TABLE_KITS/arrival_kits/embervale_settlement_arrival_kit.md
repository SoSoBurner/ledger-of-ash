# Embervale Settlement Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** Steam veils, heated halls, pipeworks, and dormer rows gather around the warmth of underground springs.

**First pressure:** overcrowding, sickness, heat-failure risk, crew debt disputes

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Embervale Settlement.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with overcrowding, sickness, heat-failure risk, crew debt disputes.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Draknor Leafward, Kairn Steelbrood, Malven Torsk, Rothen Drillwake, Torma Forgebound
