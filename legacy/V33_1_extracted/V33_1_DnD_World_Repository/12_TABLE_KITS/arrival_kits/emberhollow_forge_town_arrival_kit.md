# Emberhollow Forge Town Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** Cooled black stone terraces, open furnace yards, and rebuilt retaining walls frame the settlement.

**First pressure:** furnace failure, labor disputes, ash burial

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Emberhollow Forge Town.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with furnace failure, labor disputes, ash burial.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Morra Cindercrest, Soryn Ashvein, Talvek Ashvein
