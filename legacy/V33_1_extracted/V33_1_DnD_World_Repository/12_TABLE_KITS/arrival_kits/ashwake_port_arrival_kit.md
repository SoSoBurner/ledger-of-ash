# Ashwake Port Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** Dark quays, soot-streaked cranes, and renewal-marked public ways guide travelers through a port built to recover quickly from damage.

**First pressure:** harbor ash clogging, storm-whirlpool risk, port rivalry

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Ashwake Port.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with harbor ash clogging, storm-whirlpool risk, port rivalry.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Cindri Ashvein, Morra Ashvein, Port Factor Nemra Sootwake
