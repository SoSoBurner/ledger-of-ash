# House-level remnant claimants Faction Primer

**Identity:** House Mal Remnant Claimants

**What it wants:** Recover lost standing, settle old obligations in their favor, or resist total absorption into the Union order.

**How it operates:** document claims, patronage appeals, debt leverage, symbolic legitimacy campaigns

**What it fears:** Their residual legitimacy is strongest where records are messy and people still resent the Union settlement.

**What it is doing now:** political irrelevance, debt conversion, loss of public legitimacy

**How it reacts to party interference:** If the party helps, it will try to formalize that help into obligation. If the party obstructs it, it will answer through the same methods it uses to preserve leverage.

**Best first conflict:** A routine request becomes factional the moment the party changes pace, access, or witness control in a space this faction treats as governed.
