# Great House Citadel Circle Faction Primer

**Identity:** Great House Citadel Circle

**What it wants:** Preserve House precedence while extracting strength, loyalty, and legitimacy from volcanic endurance.

**How it operates:** house councils, forge sponsorship, oath politics, descent charters, marital and craft alliances

**What it fears:** House prestige can turn every shortage, burial, or rebuilding effort into a competition for dominance.

**What it is doing now:** succession rivalry, descent casualties, trade timing, public need for rebuilding after damage

**How it reacts to party interference:** If the party helps, it will try to formalize that help into obligation. If the party obstructs it, it will answer through the same methods it uses to preserve leverage.

**Best first conflict:** A routine request becomes factional the moment the party changes pace, access, or witness control in a space this faction treats as governed.
