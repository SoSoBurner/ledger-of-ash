# House Mimolot Faction Primer

**Identity:** House Mimolot

**Agenda feel:** Convert local stability into durable leverage through trade, law, and legitimacy.

**Authority feel:** Use institutional and locality actors for table-ready authority.

**Risk feel:** Outsiders who bypass protocol attract notice fast.

**Trade feel:** Mimolot Academy, Scriptorium Steps, Tariff Lantern Court

**Rumor feel:** Treat every favor as a future obligation and every delay as someone else’s opportunity.

**Best first conflict:** A routine request becomes factional when the party chooses whose process to honor.
