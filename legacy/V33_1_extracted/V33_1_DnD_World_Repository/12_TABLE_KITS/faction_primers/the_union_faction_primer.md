# The Union Faction Primer

**Identity:** The Union

**What it wants:** Keep trade moving, disputes arbitrated, and guild power coordinated without returning to fallen-house paralysis.

**How it operates:** trade mediation, guild negotiation, sanctioning, network infrastructure

**What it fears:** Its legitimacy rests on fairness; once people think the network is rigged, compliance decays fast.

**What it is doing now:** claims of favoritism, legacy Mal grievances, overloaded dispute channels

**How it reacts to party interference:** If the party helps, it will try to formalize that help into obligation. If the party obstructs it, it will answer through the same methods it uses to preserve leverage.

**Best first conflict:** A routine request becomes factional the moment the party changes pace, access, or witness control in a space this faction treats as governed.
