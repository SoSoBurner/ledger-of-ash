# The Principalities route hardening 2 Travel Complications

**Core route identity:** The Principalities route hardening 2

**Checkpoint problem:** Paper helps, but clear sponsorship and coherent cargo matter just as much. Now make one of those suddenly unclear.

**Patrol complication:** Routine in daylight, selective and sharper after dark. The party arrives at the wrong patrol moment.

**Night complication:** Informal power becomes more important than posted procedure after sunset. Someone offers a shortcut with a future cost.

**Seasonal pressure:** weather delay, supply interruption, inspection backlog

**Hazard twist:** Inspection pressure, rumor, and exposure risk dominate over overt environmental hazard in this packet.
