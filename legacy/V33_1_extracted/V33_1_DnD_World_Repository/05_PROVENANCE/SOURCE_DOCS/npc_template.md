# 16. ALIGNMENT-NEUTRAL PROMPTS
_Use these prompts for any NPC, including kind or heroic ones._

- What do they believe they are for?
- What part of the world do they make safer, harsher, stranger, cleaner, dirtier, holier, wealthier, or more afraid?
- What system gets better because they exist?
- What system gets worse because they exist?
- What would collapse if they disappeared tomorrow?
- What do people misunderstand about them?
- What are they using instead of healing?
- What do they protect that they should not?
- What do they refuse to protect that they should?
- What emotion do they produce in a room before they speak?
- What habit in society proves they have already changed the world?
