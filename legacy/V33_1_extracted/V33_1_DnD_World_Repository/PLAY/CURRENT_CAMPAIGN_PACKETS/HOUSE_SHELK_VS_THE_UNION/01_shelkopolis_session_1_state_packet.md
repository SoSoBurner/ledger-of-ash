The live campaign opens in **Shelkopolis** under the banner **House Shelk vs the Union**.

- **Campaign:** House Shelk vs the Union
- **Session:** 1
- **Plot point:** Prologue
- **Current objective:** Find the daughter of Lord Myrs Shelk
- **Current local location:** Shelkopolis
- **Current point of interest:** General Store
- **Weather:** Thunder storms
- **Day/Night:** Day
- **Party frame:** The Golden Crew
- **Criminality:** Wanted
- **Fame:** 2
- **Morality:** Chaotic Good

## Immediate play use
Run Session 1 as a constrained information-recovery problem, not a broad sandbox sprawl. The opening is strongest when it uses:
1. one public contact surface
2. one House Shelk gatekeeping surface
3. one Union-shadowed interpretive surface
4. one signal that delay changes who controls the story

## Best opening questions
- Who last saw the daughter under conditions that can still be verified?
- Which witness can be approached without immediately notifying the wrong faction?
- What does House Shelk want kept quiet?
- What could the Union gain if the search becomes public process instead of controlled inquiry?
