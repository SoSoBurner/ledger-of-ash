# Cosmology and Planes

The setting rests on the One splitting into shards, the pantheon emerging from that fracture, and Doomsday smearing material existence across unstable magical conditions.

## Core truths
- The material plane bisects the astral sea.
- Doomsday made arcane practice newly accessible and left world-scale scars.
- Limbo, astral transit, divine dwellings, and lower-realm pressures all matter to long-horizon play.
