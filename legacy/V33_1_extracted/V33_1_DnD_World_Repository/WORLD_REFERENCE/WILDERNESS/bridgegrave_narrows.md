# Bridgegrave Narrows

**Region:** Soreheim Alliance / Tower Bridge Underway

**Why it matters:** A suspended ruin beneath an abandoned span where discarded work camps became a black market village. Everyone there smiles like they know what your tools are worth.

**What players notice first:** A suspended ruin beneath an abandoned span where discarded work camps became a black market village.

**Current tensions:** black market, fugitives, salvage rights.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
