# Sunken Bell Garden

**Region:** Sheresh Communes / Hearthcoil Periphery

**Why it matters:** Half-frozen sculpture grounds where brass bells grow through the ice from older civic works. The tones calm some mutations and worsen others.

**What players notice first:** Half-frozen sculpture grounds where brass bells grow through the ice from older civic works.

**Current tensions:** healing experiment, cult meetings, unstable wildlife.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
