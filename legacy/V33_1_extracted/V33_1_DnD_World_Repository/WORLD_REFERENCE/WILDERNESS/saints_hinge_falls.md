# Saints Hinge Falls

**Region:** The Principalities / Panim Hinterlands

**Why it matters:** A stepped cataract where memorial goods snag in natural stone shelves below prayer paths. Pilgrims insist the water reveals what mourners tried not to admit.

**What players notice first:** A stepped cataract where memorial goods snag in natural stone shelves below prayer paths.

**Current tensions:** pilgrim traffic, relic theft, grief visions.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
