# Booktar Bog

**Region:** The Principalities / Mimolot Fringe

**Why it matters:** A black reed marsh fed by pulp runoff, lamp oil, and discarded copying fluids from tariff houses upriver. Waterproof pages drift through the muck with half-finished annotations still legible.

**What players notice first:** A black reed marsh fed by pulp runoff, lamp oil, and discarded copying fluids from tariff houses upriver.

**Current tensions:** stolen page trade, alchemical sickness, faculty blackmail.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
