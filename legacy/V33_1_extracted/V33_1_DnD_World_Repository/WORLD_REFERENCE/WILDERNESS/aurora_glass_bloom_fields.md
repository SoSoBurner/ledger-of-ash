# Aurora Glass Bloom Fields

**Region:** Sheresh Communes / Outer Waste

**Why it matters:** A frozen plain where pollution-stiffened blossoms grow in translucent clusters that chime before stormfront spell-surges. Gatherers, exile-runners, and wardens all want the field for different reasons.

**What players notice first:** A frozen plain where pollution-stiffened blossoms grow in translucent clusters that chime before stormfront spell-surges.

**Current tensions:** mutation harvest, storm-glass salvage, contamination watch.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and which local interest learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
