# Moonstamp Rest

**Region:** Nomdara Circuits / Shelk-Sheresh Frontier

**Why it matters:** A neutral encampment circle marked by layered wagon ruts and shrine posts from six cultures. Deals made here are fair only if everyone is equally tired.

**What players notice first:** A neutral encampment circle marked by layered wagon ruts and shrine posts from six cultures.

**Current tensions:** trade mediation, spies, temporary sanctuary.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
