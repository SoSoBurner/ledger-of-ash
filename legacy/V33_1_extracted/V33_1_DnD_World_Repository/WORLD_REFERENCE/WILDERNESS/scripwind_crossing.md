# Scripwind Crossing

**Region:** Nomdara Routes / High Plain Crossing

**Why it matters:** A high plain market-stop where trade promises are sung into lacquered ribbon tallies before the caravan moves on. Break the ribbon and three regions hear about it by dusk.

**What players notice first:** A high plain market-stop where trade promises are sung into lacquered ribbon tallies before the caravan moves on.

**Current tensions:** price war, contract dishonor, mercantile espionage.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
