# Widowspoke Switchyard

**Region:** The Principalities / Union Logistics Fringe

**Why it matters:** A wagon re-routing field where caravans are quietly reassigned under storm lantern code. Teamsters say no route is truly lost here, only sold twice.

**What players notice first:** A wagon re-routing field where caravans are quietly reassigned under storm lantern code.

**Current tensions:** contract fraud, missing cargo, bought witnesses.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
