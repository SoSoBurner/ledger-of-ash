# Sepulcher Tide Steps

**Region:** House Panim / Mortuary Coast

**Why it matters:** Black steps descending into a tide cave where afterlife purchases, memorial processions, and coffin-barges converge under priests who never forget a debt.

**What players notice first:** Black steps descending into a tide cave where afterlife purchases, memorial processions, and coffin-barges converge under priests who never forget a debt.

**Current tensions:** funerary traffic, debt rites, undertow danger.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and which local interest learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
