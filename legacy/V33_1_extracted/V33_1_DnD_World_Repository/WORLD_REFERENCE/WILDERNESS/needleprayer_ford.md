# Needleprayer Ford

**Region:** The Principalities / Shelk marches

**Why it matters:** A ford where devotional cairns and broken spearheads mark every crossing, making secrecy harder than danger.

**What players notice first:** A ford where devotional cairns and broken spearheads mark every crossing, making secrecy harder than danger.

**Current tensions:** pilgrim traffic, hidden watchers, wet evidence.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing here changes who learns the party passed and whether locals treat them as protected, suspicious, or marked.

**Use in play:** Put one environmental obstacle, one social read, and one consequence for being seen here before the party exits the scene.
