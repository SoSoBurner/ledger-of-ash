# Grainveil Meadows

**Region:** Principality of Zootia / Breadlands

**Why it matters:** Low, wind-combed fields that look peaceful until hidden drainage ditches, livestock drovers, and tithe patrols turn every crossing into a question of permission and yield.

**What players notice first:** Low, wind-combed fields that look peaceful until hidden drainage ditches, livestock drovers, and tithe patrols turn every crossing into a question of permission and yield.

**Current tensions:** agricultural chokepoint, drover traffic, tithe friction.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and which local interest learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
