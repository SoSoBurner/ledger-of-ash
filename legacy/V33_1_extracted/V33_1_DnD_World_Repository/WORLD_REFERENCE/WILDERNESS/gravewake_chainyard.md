# Gravewake Chainyard

**Region:** Panim war-burial approaches

**Why it matters:** A yard of anchor chains, giant bone markers, and purchased-afterlife wagons waiting for blessing windows.

**What players notice first:** A yard of anchor chains, giant bone markers, and purchased-afterlife wagons waiting for blessing windows.

**Current tensions:** funerary commerce, relic theft, clergy pressure.

**Travel consequence:** Stopping here trades speed for access to rumor, labor, and the resentments of families paying for eternity.

**Use in play:** Put one environmental obstacle, one social read, and one consequence for being seen here before the party exits the scene.
