# Frostprayer Causeway

**Region:** Sheresh Empire / Bubbleward Frontier

**Why it matters:** A snow-buried stone road that hums old choir notes through the frost whenever travelers carry active spellwork across it. Couriers use the hum to count distance; predators use it to count prey.

**What players notice first:** A snow-buried stone road that hums old choir notes through the frost whenever travelers carry active spellwork across it.

**Current tensions:** unstable cantrip echo, courier spying, mutation exposure.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
