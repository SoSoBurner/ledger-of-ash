# Votive Silt Reach

**Region:** The Principalities / Panim River Marsh

**Why it matters:** A softbank where funeral offerings and political apologies both wash ashore. Priests, thieves, and grieving families all arrive before dawn for different reasons.

**What players notice first:** A softbank where funeral offerings and political apologies both wash ashore.

**Current tensions:** mourning commerce, evidence recovery, secret meetings.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
