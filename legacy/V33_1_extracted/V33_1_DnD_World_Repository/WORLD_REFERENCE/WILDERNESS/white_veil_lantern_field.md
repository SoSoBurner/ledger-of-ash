# White Veil Lantern Field

**Region:** Sheresh Communes / Outer Ice

**Why it matters:** A snowfield studded with long-buried lantern masts whose glow wakes during contamination surges. Route wardens use the color drift to judge whether it is safe to continue.

**What players notice first:** A snowfield studded with long-buried lantern masts whose glow wakes during contamination surges.

**Current tensions:** exposure warning, salvage, false lights.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
