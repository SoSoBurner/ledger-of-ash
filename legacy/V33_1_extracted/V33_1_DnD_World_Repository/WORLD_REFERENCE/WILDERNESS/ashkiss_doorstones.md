# Ashkiss Doorstones

**Region:** Phoenix Island / Western Desert Margin

**Why it matters:** A ceremonial ring of basalt doorframes leading nowhere visible. Husbands returning with gifts sometimes stop here first to decide which truths can survive the threshold.

**What players notice first:** A ceremonial ring of basalt doorframes leading nowhere visible.

**Current tensions:** vow testing, duel site, omen stones.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
