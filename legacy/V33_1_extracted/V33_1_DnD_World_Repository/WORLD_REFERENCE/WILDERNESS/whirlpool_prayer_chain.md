# Whirlpool Prayer Chain

**Region:** Nomdara Sea Lanes / Mother Storm Approaches

**Why it matters:** A sequence of buoy shrines and weighted prayer cables marking the safest imperfect way through hostile water. Every captain claims to know the real pattern.

**What players notice first:** A sequence of buoy shrines and weighted prayer cables marking the safest imperfect way through hostile water.

**Current tensions:** navigation, pirate attention, leviathan diplomacy.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
