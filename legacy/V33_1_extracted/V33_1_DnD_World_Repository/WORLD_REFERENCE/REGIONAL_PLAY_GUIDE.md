# Regional Play Guide

## Principalities
Play this region as a web of noble mandate, guild encroachment, road policing, book tariffs, sacrifice economies, and rivalry managed through procedure, spies, and public order. Best for intrigue, law, faction pressure, and status play.

## Sheresh Communes
Play Sheresh as magical daily life under beautiful discipline. Bubble-shielded settlements, cantrip-saturated culture, artistic utility, mutation risk, and magical pollution should all be felt at street level. Best for wonder mixed with instability.

## Soreheim Alliance
Play Soreheim as scale, industry, caste logic, giant governance, and production under constant environmental threat. Towers, bridges, magma forges, and systemic interdependence define the region. Best for infrastructure drama, harsh necessity, and massive-scale travel.

## Psanan / Phoenix Island
Play this region through matriarchal household law, hardness values, gift logic, blood-vow seriousness, dangerous forests, and war-readiness. Best for ritualized gendered custom, honor, survival, and dramatic home-threshold scenes.

## Nomdara
Play Nomdara as moving leverage. It is not merely a caravan but a trade civilization with soft power, diplomacy, and coordinated market punishment. Best for mobile politics, commerce, rumor spread, and multicultural contact scenes.
