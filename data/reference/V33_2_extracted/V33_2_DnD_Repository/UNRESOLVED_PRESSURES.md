# Unresolved Pressures

## Missing daughter of Lord Myrs Shelk
- **Clock:** Fresh enough to hide, old enough to panic the household.
- **Escalates when:** witnesses are handled publicly, retainers start improvising, or the party treats rumor like evidence.

## House Shelk vs the Union
- **Clock:** Always running beneath the search.
- **Escalates when:** the problem leaves private channels and becomes a city conversation.

## Wanted party status
- **Clock:** Slow until it is suddenly immediate.
- **Escalates when:** the party uses official routes, gets named by a clerk, or asks the wrong person for urgency.

## Storm-weather timing
- **Clock:** External but decisive.
- **Escalates when:** the party waits for certainty instead of acting on narrowing windows.
