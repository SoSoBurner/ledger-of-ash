# Queenstep Red Dunes

**Region:** Phoenix Island / Interior Dune March

**Why it matters:** A red-sand crossing marked by doorway shrines where husbands returning from campaign leave blood-pricked gifts before they are judged fit to re-enter household law.

**What players notice first:** A red-sand crossing marked by doorway shrines where husbands returning from campaign leave blood-pricked gifts before they are judged fit to re-enter household law.

**Current tensions:** marriage oath disputes, gift theft, challenge duels.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
