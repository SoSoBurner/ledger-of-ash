# Embermast Hinge

**Region:** Soreheim Alliance / Tower Bridge Crown

**Why it matters:** A rotating bridge-joint the size of a district where fire-giant crews, dwarf riveters, and quota clerks all claim final authority over shutdown timing.

**What players notice first:** A rotating bridge-joint the size of a district where fire-giant crews, dwarf riveters, and quota clerks all claim final authority over shutdown timing.

**Current tensions:** production delay, caste friction, sabotage suspicion.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
