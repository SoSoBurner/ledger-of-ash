# Undertower Marrow Wells

**Region:** Soreheim Alliance / Deep Works

**Why it matters:** Heat-sunk extraction pits where mineral slurries, monster bone ash, and runoff from old forge districts settle into pale layers prized by armorers and grave-tenders alike.

**What players notice first:** Heat-sunk extraction pits where mineral slurries, monster bone ash, and runoff from old forge districts settle into pale layers prized by armorers and grave-tenders alike.

**Current tensions:** toxic runoff, illicit extraction, funerary theft.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
