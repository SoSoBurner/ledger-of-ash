# Chainlight Trestles

**Region:** Soreheim Alliance / High Span

**Why it matters:** A half-maintained bridge grid slung between tower works where signal lamps swing on chains over dizzying drops. Freight crews call it efficient; widows call it a place that eats promises.

**What players notice first:** A half-maintained bridge grid slung between tower works where signal lamps swing on chains over dizzying drops.

**Current tensions:** bridge risk, freight chokepoint, labor grievance.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and which local interest learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
