# Behir Scratch Ridge

**Region:** Soreheim Alliance / Outer Livestock Bands

**Why it matters:** A ridge of fused stone scored by repeated territorial clashes between trained behirs and anything too proud to detour. Prospectors read the grooves like weather.

**What players notice first:** A ridge of fused stone scored by repeated territorial clashes between trained behirs and anything too proud to detour.

**Current tensions:** monster territory, prospecting marker, military route.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
