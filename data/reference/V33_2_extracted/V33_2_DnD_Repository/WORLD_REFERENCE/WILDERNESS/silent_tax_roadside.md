# Silent Tax Roadside

**Region:** The Principalities / Mimolot Border Verge

**Why it matters:** A stretch of road where stamped books and confiscated copies are sometimes found stacked in perfect dry piles after rain. No border clerk admits sending them there.

**What players notice first:** A stretch of road where stamped books and confiscated copies are sometimes found stacked in perfect dry piles after rain.

**Current tensions:** book tariff scandal, evidence cache, trap site.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
