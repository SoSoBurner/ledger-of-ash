# Aurora Bone Span

**Region:** Sheresh Communes / Northern Dome March

**Why it matters:** A natural bridge of pale mineral growth arcing over a contamination trench. Children dare each other to cross at dusk when the aurora makes the span look alive.

**What players notice first:** A natural bridge of pale mineral growth arcing over a contamination trench.

**Current tensions:** crossing point, scavengers, spectacle tourism.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
