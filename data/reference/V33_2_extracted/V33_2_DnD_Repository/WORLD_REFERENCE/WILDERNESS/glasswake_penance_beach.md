# Glasswake Penance Beach

**Region:** Phoenix Island littoral

**Why it matters:** A ritual shoreline where rejected gifts, storm-thrown salvage, and family honor are sorted in public view.

**What players notice first:** A ritual shoreline where rejected gifts, storm-thrown salvage, and family honor are sorted in public view.

**Current tensions:** marital shame, storm salvage, hard-law hospitality.

**Travel consequence:** Who the party speaks to first here determines whether they are treated as petitioners, fools, or honored guests.

**Use in play:** Put one environmental obstacle, one social read, and one consequence for being seen here before the party exits the scene.
