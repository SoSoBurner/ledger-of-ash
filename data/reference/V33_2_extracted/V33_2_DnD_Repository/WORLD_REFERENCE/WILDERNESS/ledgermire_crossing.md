# Ledgermire Crossing

**Region:** The Principalities / Mimolot-Mal trade edge

**Why it matters:** A blackwater causeway where tariffs, copies, and debts seem to cling to travelers longer than mud.

**What players notice first:** A blackwater causeway where tariffs, copies, and debts seem to cling to travelers longer than mud.

**Current tensions:** book smugglers, tariff officers, vanished couriers.

**Travel consequence:** Using this crossing accelerates trade movement but creates paperwork ripples and rumor trails.

**Use in play:** Put one environmental obstacle, one social read, and one consequence for being seen here before the party exits the scene.
