# Glasshollow Mutation Drift

**Region:** Sheresh Empire / Polluted Tundra

**Why it matters:** A low basin where failed protective fields left the snow glittering with translucent organo-crystalline dust. Hunters wear mirrored scarves and do not stay after sunset.

**What players notice first:** A low basin where failed protective fields left the snow glittering with translucent organo-crystalline dust.

**Current tensions:** wild magic bloom, body horror rumors, illicit reagent harvesting.

**Travel consequence:** Crossing this site changes timing, rumor flow, and who learns the party was here.

**Use in play:** Put one resource temptation, one social witness, and one environmental risk here before the party leaves the area.
