# Routes, Travel, and Chokepoints

Travel is political. Roads, ports, tariffs, ferries, contamination lanes, tower bridges, and whirlpool-safe passages all decide who profits from movement.

## Read travel as pressure
- A storm is never just weather; it changes witness pools and messenger timing.
- A checkpoint is never just security; it decides who gets named.
- A bridge is never just infrastructure; it concentrates labor, caste, and sabotage risk.
- A caravan lane is never just distance; it carries contract memory and market leverage.
