# Changed Localities

This file now records both validated changes and active pre-promotion locality strain. The live campaign has not yet promoted a post-session structural world effect, but it has established meaningful runtime pressure at the following locations.

## Active pre-promotion locality strain

- **Locality:** Shelkopolis
  - **Change:** The city is the active runtime search ground for the daughter of Lord Myrs Shelk while House Shelk vs the Union pressure is already live.
  - **Immediate consequence:** More scrutiny around public questions, contacts, and movement tied to House Shelk interests.
  - **Long-tail consequence:** If the search stalls, Shelkopolis becomes a rumor engine rather than a controlled inquiry surface.

- **Locality:** House Shelk Manor / House of Shelk Estate
  - **Change:** The manor-estate surface is no longer a passive noble backdrop; it is an active campaign node tied to the missing-daughter objective.
  - **Immediate consequence:** Access, testimony, household routine, and guard posture all matter more than usual.
  - **Long-tail consequence:** Delay can convert internal concern into factional blame or controlled information release.

- **Locality:** General Store, Shelkopolis
  - **Change:** The live sheet places the party’s current point of interest here at the opening of the campaign.
  - **Immediate consequence:** It functions as an early contact, observation, and rumor intake point.
  - **Long-tail consequence:** If mishandled, it becomes a witness site for party reputation rather than a neutral staging point.
