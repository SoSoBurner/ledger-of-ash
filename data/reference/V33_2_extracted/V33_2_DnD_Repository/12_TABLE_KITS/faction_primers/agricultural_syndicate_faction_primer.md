# Agricultural Syndicate Faction Primer

**Identity:** Agricultural Syndicate

**What it wants:** Maintain food stability, preserve productive land, and keep communal supply obligations met.

**How it operates:** field quotas, irrigation coordination, seed and labor allocation, harvest festivals tied to contribution recognition

**What it fears:** High output expectations can turn communal planning into coercive pressure when weather or transport fails.

**What it is doing now:** seasonal flooding, predator intrusion, water-system fragility, need to keep food moving into industrial zones

**How it reacts to party interference:** If the party helps, it will try to formalize that help into obligation. If the party obstructs it, it will answer through the same methods it uses to preserve leverage.

**Best first conflict:** A routine request becomes factional the moment the party changes pace, access, or witness control in a space this faction treats as governed.
