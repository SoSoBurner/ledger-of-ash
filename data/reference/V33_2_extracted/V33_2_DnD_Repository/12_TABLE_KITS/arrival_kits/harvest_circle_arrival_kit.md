# Harvest Circle Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Open market rings, wagon circles, drying racks, and shrine lawns announce a town built for exchange as much as storage.

**First pressure:** spoilage, price pressure, favor feuds, route congestion

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Harvest Circle.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with spoilage, price pressure, favor feuds, route congestion.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Harvest Circle is covering a more expensive conflict tied to Soreheim Alliance.
