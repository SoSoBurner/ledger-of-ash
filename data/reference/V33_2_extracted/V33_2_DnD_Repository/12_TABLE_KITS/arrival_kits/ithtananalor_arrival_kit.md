# Ithtananalor Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Austere walls, disciplined checkpoints, and industrial silhouettes announce a city built for law and production.

**First pressure:** illicit magic, smuggling, public accountability for harsh enforcement

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Ithtananalor.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with illicit magic, smuggling, public accountability for harsh enforcement.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Ithtananalor is covering a more expensive conflict tied to Principality of Roaz.
