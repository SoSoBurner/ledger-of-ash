# Whitebridge Commune Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Bridgeworks, signal braziers, and snow-buried storage ribs frame the settlement.

**First pressure:** bridge icing, refugee surges, missing expeditions

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Whitebridge Commune.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with bridge icing, refugee surges, missing expeditions.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Whitebridge Commune is covering a more expensive conflict tied to Sheresh Communes.
