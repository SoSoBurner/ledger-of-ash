# Verdant Row District Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Colorful awnings, looms, hammers, flower boxes, baked bread, spiced tea, and merchant traffic.

**First pressure:** economic fraud, design theft, route security

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Verdant Row District.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with economic fraud, design theft, route security.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Verdant Row District is covering a more expensive conflict tied to Shelkopolis.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Faelar Bloomcrest
