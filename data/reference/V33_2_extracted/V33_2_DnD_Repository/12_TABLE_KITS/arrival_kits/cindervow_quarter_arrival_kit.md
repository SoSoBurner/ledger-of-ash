# Cindervow Quarter Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** Layered burn-stone walls, ash-wing banners, and engraved arches show repeated destruction and deliberate rebuilding.

**First pressure:** rebuilding backlog, house competition over visibility, structural heat stress

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Cindervow Quarter.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with rebuilding backlog, house competition over visibility, structural heat stress.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Forge Master Telan Pyrehand, Kaiva Ashvein, Vekka Cindercrest, Vessa Ashvein
