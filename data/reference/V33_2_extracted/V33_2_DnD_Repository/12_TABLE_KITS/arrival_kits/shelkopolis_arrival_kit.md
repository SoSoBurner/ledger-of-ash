# Shelkopolis Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Pale stone walls rise from meadows with golden accents, indigo and emerald banners, and flower-lined roads watched by bright-plumed Roadwardens.

**First pressure:** glyph corruption beyond the city, trade security obligations, noble rivalry, festival traffic

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Shelkopolis.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with glyph corruption beyond the city, trade security obligations, noble rivalry, festival traffic.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Shelkopolis is covering a more expensive conflict tied to Principality of Shelk.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Corin Lightloom
