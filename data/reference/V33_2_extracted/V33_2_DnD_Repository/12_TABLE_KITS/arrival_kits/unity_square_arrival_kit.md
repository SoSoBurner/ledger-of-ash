# Unity Square Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Paved exchange courts, stacked counting houses, covered loading lanes, tally towers, and color-coded guild awnings make the square feel like a civic market machine rendered in stone, wood, and cloth.

**First pressure:** price shocks, cargo theft, route closures, public rumor flareups

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Unity Square.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with price shocks, cargo theft, route closures, public rumor flareups.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Unity Square is covering a more expensive conflict tied to The Union.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Vale Brokerwell
