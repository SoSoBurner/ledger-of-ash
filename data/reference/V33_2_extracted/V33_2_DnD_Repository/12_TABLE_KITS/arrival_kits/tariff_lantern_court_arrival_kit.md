# Tariff Lantern Court Arrival Kit

**First visual:** Visitors first see tariff lamps, indexed stalls, and instructors quietly watching who touches what.

**First pressure:** tariff abuse, smuggling of notes

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, shrine intermediary, or steward appropriate to Tariff Lantern Court.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, who actually decides things here, and which mistake will cost them most.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with tariff abuse, smuggling of notes.

**What rumor surfaces:** A visible problem in Tariff Lantern Court is covering a more expensive conflict tied to House Mimolot.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Mimolot Burdened Clerk 4
