# Duneshade Outpost Arrival Kit

**First visual:** ** Low walls, shade sails, cistern towers, and signal posts mark a settlement built to survive heat, scarcity, and sudden attack.

**First pressure:** water scarcity, subterranean predators, convoy delay, desert raids and sabotage

**First official contact:** Start with a clerk, marshal, broker, dock watcher, or shrine intermediary appropriate to Duneshade Outpost.

**What players can ask:** safety, trade access, lodging, local law, current tension, and who actually decides things here.

**What goes wrong in 10 minutes:** A routine request collides with water scarcity, subterranean predators, convoy delay, desert raids and sabotage.

**What rumor surfaces:** Someone important benefits if the party solves the visible problem too quickly.

**One named NPC to anchor scene start:** Gorath Steelclad, Helga Ashmason, Hrolf Ashsight, Kraeda Tidalhart, Oryk Sarnok, Tovek Coalward, Zoreth Kaeling
