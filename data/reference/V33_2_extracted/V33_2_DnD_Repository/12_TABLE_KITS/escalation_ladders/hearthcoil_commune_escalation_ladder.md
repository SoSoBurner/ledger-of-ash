# Hearthcoil Commune Escalation Ladder

1. **Social tension:** coil failure, ration conflict, out-migration
2. **Institutional attention:** A local official narrows acceptable options and records the party's involvement.
3. **Economic consequence:** Trade, lodging, permits, or access become slower or more expensive.
4. **Movement restriction:** The party's paperwork, cargo, or local trust gets flagged.
5. **Faction escalation:** A broker, household, militia, shrine authority, or civic actor turns the problem into leverage.
