## Build doctrine
- Magic is post-rupture, cosmology-dependent, and never globally neutral.
- Magical legality is polity-specific.
- Magical practice is integrated with law, trade, sanctity, archives, infrastructure, and public fear.
- Non-damage magical consequence is often more world-shaping than raw combat output.

## Packaging rule
This build remains standalone and does not require external source references to function as a usable world package.
