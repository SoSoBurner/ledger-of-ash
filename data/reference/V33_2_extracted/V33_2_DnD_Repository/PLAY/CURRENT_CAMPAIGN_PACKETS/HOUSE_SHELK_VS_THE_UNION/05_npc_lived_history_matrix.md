This matrix converts chronology, class pressure, route dependence, and institutional memory into present-day NPC behavior. Use it to answer **why each person reacts the way they do now**, not just what role they occupy.

## GM use rules

1. Before rolling persuasion or intimidation, identify which hook the PC is pressing: **class safety, house legitimacy, religious meaning, or route control**.
2. If the party acts without sponsorship, most civilians default to **self-protective incompleteness**, not direct refusal.
3. If House Shelk loses control of sequence, the Union gains leverage by turning facts into **process**.
4. If religious framing wins first, public memory will harden around omen, shame, or trial language before evidence settles.
