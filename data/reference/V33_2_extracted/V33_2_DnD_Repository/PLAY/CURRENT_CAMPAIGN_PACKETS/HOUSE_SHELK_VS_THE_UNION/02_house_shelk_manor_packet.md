# House Shelk Manor Packet

## Canon-safe identity note
The live campaign sheet uses **House Shelk Manor** as an active location surface. The baseline repository’s directly asserted noble site is **House of Shelk Estate** in Shelkopolis. For current campaign play, treat “House Shelk Manor” as the table-facing manor-estate surface of the House of Shelk Estate rather than as a newly invented separate settlement.

## What the manor packet is for
Use this packet when the party moves from city-level search play into household access, family control, guard filtering, servant testimony, or private shame-management.

## Best scene functions
- restricted entry negotiation
- household witness interviews
- servant-corridor rumor intake
- patronage leverage
- quiet threat without public spectacle

## Visible power
- House Shelk household authority
- retained guards and military advisers
- archival and domestic staff who control access through routine

## Active tensions
- missing-daughter pressure
- information containment
- factional embarrassment risk
- outsiders being useful versus dangerous
