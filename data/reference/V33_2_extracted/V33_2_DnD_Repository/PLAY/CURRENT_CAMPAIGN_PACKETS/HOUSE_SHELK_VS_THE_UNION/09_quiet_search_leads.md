# Quiet Search Leads

These leads are built for a search that stays social, deniable, and fast.

## 1. The dry ledger in the wet shop
A store clerk kept one page dry during the storm. The page is not important as accounting, but it proves who came in calm enough to avoid rain.

**Reveals:** someone arrived before the worst weather and expected to wait.

## 2. The servant who is loyal to dignity, not truth
A House servant will protect Lord Myrs Shelk's standing even if it costs hours. They are not the traitor. They are the delay.

**Reveals:** one lead was quietly redirected away from a socially dangerous name.

## 3. The carriage rug that should not be clean
One carriage claims not to have moved, but its floor covering was shaken out recently.

**Reveals:** a household or guild vehicle made a short discreet run.

## 4. The Union favor that arrives too early
A Union-aligned fixer offers exactly the right help before the party publicly asks for it.

**Reveals:** somebody is monitoring the pace of the search, not just the outcome.

## 5. The storm-gap witness
One witness only saw silhouettes, but they know who moved like staff and who moved like someone pretending to be staff.

**Reveals:** the disappearance touched class behavior and costume, not just location.

## Best use order
1. shop pressure
2. servant pressure
3. transport pressure
4. Union pressure
5. witness confirmation
