# Ashforge Citadel quickstart card

**Polity:** Psanan
**Visual read:** Black-red ramparts, forge smoke, and ash-banner civic markers announce a city accustomed to heat, rebuilding, and rule.
**Behavioral read:** respect House precedence; treat rebuilding markers and dedication fires as public obligations
**Danger read:** house rivalry; forge accidents; ashfall damage
**Who matters first:** Great House Citadel Circle, Forge Dedication Halls, Rebuilding Oath Courts
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Ashforge Citadel immediately highlights central fortified seat of psanan where great house rule, forge prestige, and civic renewal ceremony visibly converge
