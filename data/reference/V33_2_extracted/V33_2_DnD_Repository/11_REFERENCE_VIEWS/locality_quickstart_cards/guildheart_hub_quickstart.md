# Guildheart Hub quickstart card

**Polity:** The Union
**Visual read:** Multi-story counting halls, bonded warehouse blocks, canal-side loading lanes, registry towers, and arbitration pavilions announce that cargo paper, inspection rhythm, and freight infrastructure rule this place.
**Behavioral read:** declare business plainly; observe queue and hearing order; produce records when challenged; respect sanction notices
**Danger read:** legacy Mal claims; smuggling; Red Hood rumor presence; imperial oversight
**Who matters first:** The Union, Guild Sanction Board, merchant houses
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Guildheart Hub immediately highlights dense industrial-urban nerve center of the union successor order where contract legitimacy, merchant standing, tariff politics, and freight administration are made visible
