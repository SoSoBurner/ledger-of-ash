# Starbreak Commune quickstart card

**Polity:** Sheresh Communes
**Visual read:** Tall ice-sheathed towers and mirrored sighting plates rise above the dome rim.
**Behavioral read:** honor launch windows; record returns and non-returns accurately
**Danger read:** crew attrition; signal blackout; observation exposure
**Who matters first:** Route Warden Compacts, Containment Research Concord
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Starbreak Commune immediately highlights a high-risk sheresh commune built around sky observation, long-range signaling, and expedition launch cycles
