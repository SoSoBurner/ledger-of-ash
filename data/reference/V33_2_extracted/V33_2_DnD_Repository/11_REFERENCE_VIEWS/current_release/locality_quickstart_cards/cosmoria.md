# Cosmoria

**Polity:** House Cosmouth

**Arrival:**** Grand libraries and maritime infrastructure define first impressions.

**Inside:**** Archives, tax records, and shipwright activity mingle with cosmopolitan floating-city life.

**Economic role:** shipwright industry, archives, maritime trade

**Law and enforcement feel:** archive and tax discipline

**Religious and cultural texture:** sea communion and noble retreat culture present nearby

**Active tensions:** storm weather, trade vulnerability

**Named NPC slice:** Aurek Tidereach, Coral Jack Neris, Coralyn Foamglass, High Tide-Priest Coren Mirthwake, Ilyra Foamveil, Maris Coralwake, Nerissa Bluefin, Niv Brinegrin

**Linked routes:** Cosmoria - Brineland harbor ring

**World-bridge block:** Cosmoria matters because it anchors House Cosmouth in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
