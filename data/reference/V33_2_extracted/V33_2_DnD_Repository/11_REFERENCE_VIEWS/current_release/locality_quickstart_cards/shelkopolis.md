# Shelkopolis

**Polity:** Principality of Shelk

**Arrival:**** Pale stone walls rise from meadows with golden accents, indigo and emerald banners, and flower-lined roads watched by bright-plumed Roadwardens.

**Inside:**** Curving smooth streets link elegant upper quarters, busy workshop districts, and purposeful lower wards. Textiles, tools, shrines, taverns, and scholars give the city constant motion.

**Economic role:** fashion production, enchanted tailoring, merchant exchange, craft education, Roadwarden support industry

**Law and enforcement feel:** Fashion and decorum law intensify in noble and ceremonial spaces., Intellectual property and espionage law are serious in elite and archival sites.

**Religious and cultural texture:** Compassion, contemplation, renewal, and fear each have visible public spaces and offerings.

**Active tensions:** glyph corruption beyond the city, trade security obligations, noble rivalry, festival traffic

**Named NPC slice:** Corin Lightloom, Elandra Sunweave, Maelin Dawnmantle, Maelis Starbloom, Maelis Threadmere, Mother Eliane Threadmercy, Ser Caldrin Vey, Sera Aurorine

**Linked routes:** Fairhaven - Shelkopolis road, Shelkopolis - Fairhaven road, Shelkopolis–Plume’s End Northern Road, Shelkopolis - Plume’s End northern road

**World-bridge block:** Shelkopolis matters because it anchors Principality of Shelk in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
