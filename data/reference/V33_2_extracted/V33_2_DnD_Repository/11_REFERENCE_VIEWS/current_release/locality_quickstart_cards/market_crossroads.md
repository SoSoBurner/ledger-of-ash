# Market Crossroads

**Polity:** The Union

**Arrival:**** Wheel-rut geometry, ferry callouts, stacked relay barns, bonded storehouses, and cramped loading courts make it obvious that nothing here exists without movement and managed turnover.

**Inside:**** The settlement breathes in waves of arrival, unloading, short-term lodging, warehousing, and renegotiated destination amid dense carrier traffic and clerical oversight.

**Economic role:** transshipment, storage, lodging, haulage matching

**Law and enforcement feel:** Transit fees, manifest records, and storage liability define public law here.

**Religious and cultural texture:** Roadside shrines survive because movement is dangerous and delay is expensive.

**Active tensions:** route bottlenecks, bandit intelligence leakage, cargo spoilage, licensed smuggling
