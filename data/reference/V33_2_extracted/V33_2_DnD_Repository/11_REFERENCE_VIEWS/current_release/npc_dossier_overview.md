# NPC Dossier Overview

The active release now includes 677 named NPC dossiers. Each dossier preserves asserted canon while extending the NPC into an immediately playable life surface: where they stand, what pressures shape them, how they are likely to be encountered, who they lean on, and what kind of trouble follows them into the scene.

Use dossier surfaces when a locality card gives you the where, but you still need the human engine that makes the place behave.
