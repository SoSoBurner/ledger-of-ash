# Sheresh Communes

**Summary:** Humans, Elves, and Halflings are resolved as the core, with Half-Elves retained as a conservative adjacency-based secondary race.

**Hierarchy parent:** Material Planet

**Key localities:** Aurora Crown Commune, Glasswake Commune, Hearthcoil Commune, Starbreak Commune, Whitebridge Commune

**Power ecology:** Arbiter Nyra Thawmark, Aster Aurorine, Aster Frostglass, Aster Rimebridge, Aster Snowveil, Aster Starice

**Table posture:** Prepare this polity through its corridors, gate routines, and locality tensions rather than treating it as a lore blob.
