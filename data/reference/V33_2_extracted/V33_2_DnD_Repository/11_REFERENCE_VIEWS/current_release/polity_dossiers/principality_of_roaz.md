# Principality of Roaz

**Summary:** Humans remain primary, while Half-Elves, Elves, and Gnomes align with Roazian law, industry, and anti-magic technical culture.

**Hierarchy parent:** The Principalities

**Key localities:** Iron Ledger Ward, Ironhold Quarry, Ithtananalor, Silent Haven

**Power ecology:** Use institutional and locality actors for table-ready authority.

**Table posture:** Prepare this polity through its corridors, gate routines, and locality tensions rather than treating it as a lore blob.
