# GM Deep World Guide

This guide is a derived GM-facing view subordinate to canon. It emphasizes operational truth, contradiction-awareness, and runtime support.

## Core GM principle
Most scenes should be run from locality pressure, not from abstract lore recall. Start with place hierarchy, weather/season, visible power, expected conduct, and what pressure is currently pushing downward.

## Institutional texture
- Shelk: decorum and fashion law intensify around noble and ceremonial spaces.
- Roaz: process, contraband pressure, antimagic legitimacy, and enforcement discipline shape civic interaction.
- Panim: funerary trust and ritual integrity make grief administratively consequential.
- Soreheim: quotas, production, and communal surveillance create pressure even when nobody is openly hostile.
- Sheresh: magical contamination and dome stability make curiosity a managed risk.
- Psanan: house authority, volcanic hazard, and route scarcity make severity rational rather than ornamental.

## Contradiction handling
Do not flatten divine contradiction. The Vonalzo/Remeny package and related memory or seed distortions should be preserved as contradiction-aware truth instead of harmonized into a falsely clean history.

## Good runtime questions
- What does this place expect before it offers anything?
- Who benefits from public order here?
- What failure would embarrass the institution in charge?
- Which pressure is being hidden badly enough for outsiders to notice?
- What does breaking a small norm tell the table about breaking a major one?
