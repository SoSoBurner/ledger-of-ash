# Active Factions

## House Shelk
- **Public posture:** Controlled concern and procedural dignity.
- **Private need:** Recover the daughter before household authority starts looking porous.
- **How they pressure the party:** By making access conditional on discretion, loyalty signals, and quick visible competence.
- **How they may break:** Internal blame, servant panic, or a retainer deciding self-protection matters more than truth.

## The Union
- **Public posture:** Trade legitimacy, practical order, and plausible distance.
- **Private need:** A House Shelk stumble big enough to echo through roads, contracts, and urban rumor.
- **How they pressure the party:** Through delay, offers, witnesses, transport friction, and people who profit when help arrives with terms.
- **How they may break:** If the matter is resolved before they can narrate it.

## Roadwardens
- **Public posture:** Order, roads, and movement legibility.
- **Private need:** To avoid a patrol-side embarrassment during bad weather.
- **How they pressure the party:** Documenting movement, demanding legible purpose, and turning attention into cost.

## Household Retainers and Manor Staff
- **Public posture:** Loyal silence.
- **Private need:** To survive the aftermath without becoming the easiest scapegoat.
- **How they pressure the party:** Partial truths, redirected blame, nervous cooperation, and social tests disguised as service.
