Ledger of Ash is a text-first, route-driven dark fantasy RPG for browser.

You begin as a capable person in an unstable world. The eastern route has gone wrong. 
Thirty-one missing persons. Someone knows what happened. The world is still moving 
while you stand there deciding what to do about it.

FEATURES
• 31 archetypes across 4 class families — each with distinct Stage I gameplay paths
• 93 backgrounds — every origin is a different way into the same living world
• 124 consequence nodes — branching investigation with real archetype differentiation
• 4 recruitable companions — world-rooted, naturally encountered, any build can find them
• Living world — faction clocks, rival adventurers, world notices, dynamic scene text
• Party system — trust thresholds, camp talk, companion abilities, leave conditions
• First-class combat — encounter setup, enemy intent, ability selection, retreat options
• Journal with typed records — deduplication, categories, locality stamps
• Stage I–II progression — grassroots origins widen into regional consequence
• Hall of Legends — your completed runs are permanently remembered
• Browser-playable — no install, no server, works on desktop and mobile

THE WORLD
Based on V28_8 Material Planet canon. Eleven playable localities, eight distinct 
polities, fifteen canonical factions. The world keeps moving whether you act or not.

STAGE I — Grassroots
Your local situation. Intimate, survival-oriented, socially grounded.
Each background has a distinct opening path before the shared investigation emerges.

STAGE II — Regional Name  
Your reputation precedes you. Denser, more factional, more dangerous, more public.
Travel opens. Stakes widen. The world has noticed what you've been doing.
