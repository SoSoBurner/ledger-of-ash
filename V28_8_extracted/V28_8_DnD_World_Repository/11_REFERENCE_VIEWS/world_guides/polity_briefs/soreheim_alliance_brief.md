## Faith in practice
State religion reinforces ambition, domination, contemplation, and communal purpose through integrated ceremonial life.

## Law and consequence texture
Law is hierarchical, quota-conscious, and restorative in some domains but severe toward sabotage, disloyalty, and illicit magic.

## Economy and movement
The Eternal Lands, Unternal Lands, and Soreheim Proper form an interdependent extraction-production-refinement economy.

Contribution, ambition, industrial discipline, and communal obligation define everyday social value.
