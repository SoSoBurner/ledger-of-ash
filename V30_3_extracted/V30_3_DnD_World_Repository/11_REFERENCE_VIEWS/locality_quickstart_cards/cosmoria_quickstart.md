# Cosmoria quickstart card

**Polity:** Principality of Cosmouth
**Visual read:** Grand libraries and maritime infrastructure define first impressions.
**Behavioral read:** respect archives and maritime administration
**Danger read:** storm weather; trade vulnerability
**Who matters first:** House Cosmouth
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Cosmoria immediately highlights floating intellectual and shipwright metropolis of cosmouth
