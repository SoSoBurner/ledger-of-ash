# Aurora Crown Commune quickstart card

**Polity:** Sheresh Communes
**Visual read:** Blue-white dome glow, layered ice walls, and guarded thermal inlets announce a commune built to keep life possible.
**Behavioral read:** respect dome protocols; report cracks, contamination, and supply irregularities immediately
**Danger read:** dome stress; contamination seepage; ration balancing
**Who matters first:** Sheresh Dome Stewards, Containment Research Concord
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Aurora Crown Commune immediately highlights largest sheresh dome commune, where survival administration, celestial study, and public endurance ritual converge
