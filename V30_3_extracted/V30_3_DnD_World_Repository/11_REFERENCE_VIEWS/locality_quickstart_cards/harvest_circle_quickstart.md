# Harvest Circle quickstart card

**Polity:** Soreheim Alliance
**Visual read:** Open market rings, wagon circles, drying racks, and shrine lawns announce a town built for exchange as much as storage.
**Behavioral read:** declare loads honestly; respect family-controlled stalls and convoy order; treat festival exchange as civic duty as well as profit
**Danger read:** spoilage; price pressure; favor feuds; route congestion
**Who matters first:** harvest stewards, quota clerks, patron-families
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Harvest Circle immediately highlights cultural and market town where harvest patron-families, quota clerks, and festival brokers turn field output into alliance redistribution
