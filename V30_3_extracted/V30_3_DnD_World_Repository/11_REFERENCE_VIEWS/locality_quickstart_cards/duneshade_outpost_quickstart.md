# Duneshade Outpost quickstart card

**Polity:** Soreheim Alliance
**Visual read:** Low walls, shade sails, cistern towers, and signal posts mark a settlement built to survive heat, scarcity, and sudden attack.
**Behavioral read:** report hazards quickly; respect ration order; show deference to whichever family or captain controls your shelter lane
**Danger read:** water scarcity; subterranean predators; convoy delay; desert raids and sabotage
**Who matters first:** route captains, rationing families, frontier stewards
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Duneshade Outpost immediately highlights fortified hot-edge outpost where route captains, water-ration families, and predator-watch crews hold the desertward lanes of the unternal supply belt
