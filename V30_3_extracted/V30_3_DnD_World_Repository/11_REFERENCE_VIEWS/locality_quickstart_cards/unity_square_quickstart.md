# Unity Square quickstart card

**Polity:** The Union
**Visual read:** Paved exchange courts, stacked counting houses, covered loading lanes, tally towers, and color-coded guild awnings make the square feel like a civic market machine rendered in stone, wood, and cloth.
**Behavioral read:** state terms clearly; observe inspection lines; treat posted rates and guild colors as operative facts
**Danger read:** price shocks; cargo theft; route closures; public rumor flareups
**Who matters first:** Union market stewards, arbitration runners, licensed guild delegates
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Unity Square immediately highlights high-density urban exchange quarter where negotiated trade replaces inherited privilege as the public language of status, procedure, and access
