# Soreheim Proper quickstart card

**Polity:** Soreheim Alliance
**Visual read:** Multiple colossal Titan Towers rise across volcanic industry, bridged by heat-hazed logistics and treaty traffic.
**Behavioral read:** meet quotas; respect tower rank; treat decrees, seals, and work assignments as binding
**Danger read:** quota demands; political rivalry; foreign pressure; industrial accidents; tower maintenance strain
**Who matters first:** Giant Council, Cloud Giant governance cadres, tower tribunals
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Soreheim Proper immediately highlights harsh hierarchical tower-centered production core where alliance governance, foreign affairs, war output, and high-order manufacturing converge across many titan towers
