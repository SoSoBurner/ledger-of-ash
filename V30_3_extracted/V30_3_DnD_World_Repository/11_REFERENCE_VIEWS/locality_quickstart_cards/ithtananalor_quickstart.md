# Ithtananalor quickstart card

**Polity:** Principality of Roaz
**Visual read:** Austere walls, disciplined checkpoints, and industrial silhouettes announce a city built for law and production.
**Behavioral read:** comply promptly; accept inspection; treat law as public culture rather than negotiable custom
**Danger read:** illicit magic; smuggling; public accountability for harsh enforcement
**Who matters first:** Lord Aric Roaz, Office of Roazian Enforcement, Iron Accord
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Ithtananalor immediately highlights fortified administrative heart of roaz, where legal identity, industry, and enforcement culture visibly merge
