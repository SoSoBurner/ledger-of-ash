# Ashforge Quarry quickstart card

**Polity:** Soreheim Alliance
**Visual read:** High walls, ore lifts, beacon chains, and blackened roadbeds cut through a rocky valley alive with hauling noise and watch calls.
**Behavioral read:** contribute or clear the lane; respect foremen, signal calls, and family claims over crews and bunks
**Danger read:** predators; avalanches; geothermal bursts; sabotage; crew rivalries
**Who matters first:** Hill Giant foremen, industrial clans, regional bosses
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Ashforge Quarry immediately highlights fortified quarry-settlement where ore extraction, partial refinement, predator defense, and hard clan-family patronage dominate daily survival
