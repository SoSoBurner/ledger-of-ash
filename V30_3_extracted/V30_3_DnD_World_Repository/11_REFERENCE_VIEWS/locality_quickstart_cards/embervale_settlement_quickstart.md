# Embervale Settlement quickstart card

**Polity:** Soreheim Alliance
**Visual read:** Steam veils, heated halls, pipeworks, and dormer rows gather around the warmth of underground springs.
**Behavioral read:** respect shift rotation; honor bunk claims and ration order; clear debts before asking for shelter favors
**Danger read:** overcrowding; sickness; heat-failure risk; crew debt disputes
**Who matters first:** regional quartermasters, shift-boss families, repair captains
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Embervale Settlement immediately highlights geothermal labor haven where recovering crews, repair workers, and shift-boss families regroup between tower-expansion and quarry assignments
