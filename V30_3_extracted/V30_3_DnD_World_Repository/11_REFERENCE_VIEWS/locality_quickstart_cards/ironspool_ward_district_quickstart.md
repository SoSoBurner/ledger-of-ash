# Ironspool Ward District quickstart card

**Polity:** Shelkopolis
**Visual read:** Harsher edges, cheaper stalls, and visible repair culture replace elite polish.
**Behavioral read:** mind your business, pay your tab, and do not mistake poverty for disorder
**Danger read:** desperation contracts; petty crime; omens and superstition
**Who matters first:** local warders, repair guilds, tavern rumor-brokers
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Ironspool Ward District immediately highlights rougher labor and sellsword quarter of affordable goods, repair shops, and low-end taverns
