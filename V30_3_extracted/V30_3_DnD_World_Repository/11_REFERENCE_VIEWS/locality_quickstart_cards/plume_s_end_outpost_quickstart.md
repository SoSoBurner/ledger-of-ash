# Plume’s End Outpost quickstart card

**Polity:** Principality of Shelk
**Visual read:** Rugged but orderly roadside compound with tavern, armory, waystation, and shrine.
**Behavioral read:** submit to route order; respect shrine etiquette; state business clearly
**Danger read:** caravan disruption rumors; celestial enforcer sightings
**Who matters first:** Roadwardens and caravan authorities
**Easiest playable complication:** A minor official wants paperwork clarified.
**Best opener:** A first arrival at Plume’s End Outpost immediately highlights northern road outpost guarding and servicing caravans between shelkopolis and fairhaven
