# Soreheim Alliance dossier

**Entry feel:** High walls, ore lifts, beacon chains, and blackened roadbeds cut through a rocky valley alive with hauling noise and watch calls.
**Dominant pressure pattern:** predators
**Common outsider mistake:** Treating Ashforge Quarry like a casual stop rather than a governed space.
**How power really moves:** Through Hill Giant foremen, industrial clans.
**Best first three nodes to stage:** Ashforge Quarry, Duneshade Outpost, Embervale Settlement
**Named NPC depth:** 73 records tied to this polity in V30_3.
**Play use:** Start with arrival pressure, move to a fixer or authority contact, then escalate into trade, law, or ritual consequence.
