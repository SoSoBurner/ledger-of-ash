# Sunspire Haven

**Polity:** Soreheim Alliance

**Arrival:**** Broad roads, cart yards, workshops, and counting sheds gather around a busy market heart in productive country.

**Inside:**** Tool halls, textile rows, grain brokers, contract tables, and shared storehouses crowd the center while family compounds and worker yards ring the lanes.

**Economic role:** tools, textiles, market exchange, grain routing, labor brokerage

**Law and enforcement feel:** communal ownership norms enforced through regional decrees, syndicate records, and family obligation

**Religious and cultural texture:** harvest and unity rites sit beside practical oath-rituals for contracts, storage, and convoy departure

**Active tensions:** predators, water system vulnerabilities, storage disputes, family favor feuds

**Named NPC slice:** Garren Forgebound, Kael Emberthrone, Orvak Strone, Taldan Veyst, Torma Coalward, Varik Drillwake

**Linked routes:** Sunspire Haven - Harvest Circle road, Sunspire Haven - Ironroot Crossing freight lane

**World-bridge block:** Sunspire Haven matters because it anchors Soreheim Alliance in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
