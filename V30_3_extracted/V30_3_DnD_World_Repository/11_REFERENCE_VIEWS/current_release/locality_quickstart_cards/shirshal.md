# Shirshal

**Polity:** House Shirsh

**Arrival:**** Arcane scrutiny and guarded curiosity.

**Inside:**** Cases, magical anomalies, and private investigations drive civic motion.

**Economic role:** investigation, magical research

**Law and enforcement feel:** magical compliance

**Religious and cultural texture:** secondary to investigative mandate

**Active tensions:** magical law violations

**Named NPC slice:** Examiner Prelate Sira Doveshade, Hel Brenn, Measurer Seln Archive, Mercy-Examiner Talan Vey, Nira Veilthorn, Pell Rookglass, Strategos Nima Glass-Law, Veyn Veilthorn

**Linked routes:** Shirshal - Crystal Grove route

**World-bridge block:** Shirshal matters because it anchors House Shirsh in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
