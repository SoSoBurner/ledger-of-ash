# Panim Haven

**Polity:** House Panim

**Arrival:**** Temples, ritual logistics, and funerary commerce shape the city.

**Inside:**** Offerings, mediation, coffins, and divine-balance services dominate.

**Economic role:** afterlife preparations, offerings, mediation

**Law and enforcement feel:** sacrificial and mediation decorum

**Religious and cultural texture:** very high

**Active tensions:** divine balance obligations

**Named NPC slice:** Celis Lanthorn, Ishra Lumenveil, Marrowen Mercycrypt, Mediator Selka Var, Ossarch Veyn Halcyon, Pelor Coffinmarch, Registrar-Confessor Othan Mire, Saint-Physician Orel Vaunt

**Linked routes:** Panim Haven - Sanctum Sands memorial road

**World-bridge block:** Panim Haven matters because it anchors House Panim in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
