# Tideglass Registry

**Polity:** House Cosmouth

**Arrival:**** Floating berths, signal towers, and ledger pontoons make entry feel audited before welcomed.

**Inside:**** Inside, clerks, pilots, and shipwright factors move between buoyed stacks of records and wet-dock authority.

**Economic role:** salvage arbitration, ship registration, repair trade

**Law and enforcement feel:** Local expectations are enforced first socially, then institutionally.

**Religious and cultural texture:** sea communion, oath rituals

**Active tensions:** storm disruptions, fraudulent registry claims
