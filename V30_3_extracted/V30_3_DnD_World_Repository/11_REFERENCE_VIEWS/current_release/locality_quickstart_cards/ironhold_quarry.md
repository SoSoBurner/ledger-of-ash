# Ironhold Quarry

**Polity:** Principality of Roaz

**Arrival:**** Severe extraction landscape with visible labor discipline and transport controls.

**Inside:**** Ore flows, guarded labor, and infrastructure support make the quarry a blunt expression of Roazian order.

**Economic role:** ore, stone, strategic construction resources

**Law and enforcement feel:** high enforcement visibility

**Religious and cultural texture:** Low-ornament discipline and civic necessity dominate.

**Active tensions:** labor harshness, resource theft, border competition

**Named NPC slice:** Petra Stonewake, Rhodra Boltmere

**Linked routes:** Ithtananalor - Ironhold Quarry road, Principality of Roaz route hardening 3

**World-bridge block:** Ironhold Quarry matters because it anchors Principality of Roaz in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
