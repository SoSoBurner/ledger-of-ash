# Maskscar Plaza District

**Polity:** Psanan

**Arrival:**** Wide plazas, mosaic routes, and workshop facades show a settlement designed for processional movement.

**Inside:**** Mask workshops, banner halls, and rehearsal terraces make ceremony a labor system as well as a public identity.

**Economic role:** ceremonial production, public event logistics, ash dye

**Law and enforcement feel:** public route law, ceremonial property protection

**Religious and cultural texture:** explicitly civic, seasonal, dynastic, and ceremonial rather than temple-centered

**Active tensions:** house rivalry over symbolism, crowd control, heat damage to stored materials

**Named NPC slice:** Istran Ashvein, Vessa Cindercrest

**Linked routes:** No direct corridor packet resolved; use regional approach context.

**World-bridge block:** Maskscar Plaza District matters because it anchors Psanan in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
