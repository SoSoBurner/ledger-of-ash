# Craftspire

**Polity:** The Union

**Arrival:**** Tall workhouses, copy towers, materials yards, ledger balconies, and cart-choked service lanes rise around a district where skilled labor and regulated output become visible prestige.

**Inside:**** The district hums with cutters, binders, copyists, enchanters, inspectors, and porters moving through dense multi-level production spaces tied directly to storage and dispatch systems.

**Economic role:** book copying, regulated magical trade, artisan showcase production, warehouse integration

**Law and enforcement feel:** Book-tax and copy-right enforcement are visible and economically central.

**Religious and cultural texture:** Practical devotion survives in workshop alcoves and opening rites before major work runs.

**Active tensions:** copy theft, intellectual property disputes, material shortages, sabotage

**Named NPC slice:** Jorin Ledgermere, Tess Ledgermere, Union Burdened Clerk 4, Union Dockside or Street Fixer 5, Union Factor 2, Union Gate Marshal 1, Union Licensed Appraiser 8, Union Ritual Custodian 3

**Linked routes:** Guildheart Hub - Craftspire workshop corridor, The Union route corridor 1, The Union route corridor 2, The Union route corridor 3

**World-bridge block:** Craftspire matters because it anchors The Union in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
