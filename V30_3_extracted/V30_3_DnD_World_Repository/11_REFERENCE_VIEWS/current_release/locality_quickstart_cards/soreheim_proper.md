# Soreheim Proper

**Polity:** Soreheim Alliance

**Arrival:**** Multiple colossal Titan Towers rise across volcanic industry, bridged by heat-hazed logistics and treaty traffic.

**Inside:**** Tower-bases burn with magma forges, mid-levels pulse with workshops and arsenals, and upper crowns hold governance chambers, observatories, archives, and foreign delegations.

**Economic role:** high-order manufacturing, war production, refinement, distribution, foreign trade oversight

**Law and enforcement feel:** communal ownership and restorative justice enforced through central tower review, domestic patronage in other regions never supersedes Soreheim Proper decree

**Religious and cultural texture:** ambition, domination, and retribution saturate tower ritual, oath displays, and public merit judgment

**Active tensions:** quota demands, political rivalry, foreign pressure, industrial accidents, tower maintenance strain

**Named NPC slice:** Brala Coalward, Dema Forgebound, Eryndor Bladewright, Helga Ironslope, Kordr Vulkhand, Korin Drillwake, Lyria Firesoul, Mordoth Valinheim

**Linked routes:** Soreheim Proper - Eternal Lands freight route, Soreheim Proper - Ironroot Crossing

**World-bridge block:** Soreheim Proper matters because it anchors Soreheim Alliance in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
