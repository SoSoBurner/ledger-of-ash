# Witness Coil

**Polity:** House Shirsh

**Arrival:**** Approach is quiet, intentional, and lined with observation posts disguised as civic decor.

**Inside:**** Inside, examiners, wardens, and petitioners move with the caution of people who expect words to be tested.

**Economic role:** case review, forensic magic, licensed inquiry

**Law and enforcement feel:** Local expectations are enforced first socially, then institutionally.

**Religious and cultural texture:** thin and secondary

**Active tensions:** false testimony, arcane contamination

**Named NPC slice:** Shirsh Burdened Clerk 4, Shirsh Dockside or Street Fixer 5, Shirsh Factor 2, Shirsh Gate Marshal 1, Shirsh Licensed Appraiser 8, Shirsh Ritual Custodian 3, Shirsh Unstable Broker 6, Shirsh Watch Lieutenant 7

**Linked routes:** Principality of Shirsh route corridor 1, Principality of Shirsh route corridor 2, Principality of Shirsh route corridor 3

**World-bridge block:** Witness Coil matters because it anchors House Shirsh in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
