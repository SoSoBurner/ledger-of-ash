# Harvest Circle

**Polity:** Soreheim Alliance

**Arrival:**** Open market rings, wagon circles, drying racks, and shrine lawns announce a town built for exchange as much as storage.

**Inside:**** Granaries, counting courts, communal tables, festival grounds, and family compounds share one busy civic center.

**Economic role:** grain redistribution, festival trade, storage, convoy assembly

**Law and enforcement feel:** market peace is enforced through communal law, clerk records, and family responsibility for stall, storage, and convoy conduct

**Religious and cultural texture:** unity and harvest rites are public, crowded, and used to reset debts, favors, and work pledges

**Active tensions:** spoilage, price pressure, favor feuds, route congestion

**Named NPC slice:** Elyra Mossbane, Farlan Inkshade, Garren Ashmason, Jorva Helmrune, Svala Coalward, Varik Stonecarve, Velrik Durnshade

**Linked routes:** Sunspire Haven - Harvest Circle road

**World-bridge block:** Harvest Circle matters because it anchors Soreheim Alliance in the wider world, carries pressure beyond its own streets, and can redirect trade, law, rumor, and campaign movement if destabilized.
