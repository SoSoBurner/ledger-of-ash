# Soreheim Alliance

**Summary:** Giant-led alliance ruled from Soreheim Proper, where central quota authority, industrial hierarchy, and regional mediation hold together a large multi-population state.

**Hierarchy parent:** Material Planet

**Key localities:** Ashforge Quarry, Duneshade Outpost, Embervale Settlement, Harvest Circle, Soreheim Proper, Sunspire Haven

**Power ecology:** Borrik Spanmender, Brakka Stonewake, Brala Coalward, Brala Ironslope, Cron Udenine, Decon Von Reckshem

**Table posture:** Prepare this polity through its corridors, gate routines, and locality tensions rather than treating it as a lore blob.
