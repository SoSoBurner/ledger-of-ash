# Principality of Shelk

**Summary:** Elves remain primary, with Half-Elves, Humans, Gnomes, and Firbolgs retained as the best-supported extension of Shelk’s urban, artistic, and nature-linked identity.

**Hierarchy parent:** The Principalities

**Key localities:** Emberforge Outpost, Fairhaven, Plume’s End Outpost, Shelkopolis

**Power ecology:** Use institutional and locality actors for table-ready authority.

**Table posture:** Prepare this polity through its corridors, gate routines, and locality tensions rather than treating it as a lore blob.
