# The Union

**Summary:** Humans and Gnomes remain primary; Half-Elves, Elves, and Yuan-Ti are formalized as durable resident blocs in the exchange and mediation core.

**Hierarchy parent:** The Principalities

**Key localities:** Arbiters Row, Craftspire, Guildheart Hub, Ledger Canal, Market Crossroads, Mediators Court, Unity Square

**Power ecology:** Arin March, Calia Brokerwell, Calia Ledgermere, Guild Auditor Bren Oss, Guilden Ledgermere, Halv Ledgermere

**Table posture:** Prepare this polity through its corridors, gate routines, and locality tensions rather than treating it as a lore blob.
